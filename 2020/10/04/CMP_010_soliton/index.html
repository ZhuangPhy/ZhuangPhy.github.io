经典波动方程中的孤子

波包的速度是群速度决定的（色散关系 \(\omega=\pm \sqrt{v_{0}^{2} k^{2}+m^{2}}\)） \[ v_{g}=\frac{\partial \omega}{\partial k}=\pm \frac{v_{0}^{2} k}{\sqrt{v_{0}^{2} k^{2}+m^{2}}} \] 不同波矢 \(k=2 \pi / \lambda\) 的成分具有不同的传播速度。一个波包在传播过程中会分解成一个个不同波矢的子波，弥散开，这就是色散效应。

而孤立波（solitary wave）是一种在传播过程中能量不发散的波动。

例子：孤子的能量不发散

我们以一个形象的例子来展示什么是能量不发散的波动？为什么可以看成一个粒子？

运动方程 \[ \frac{d^{2} \phi}{d t^{2}}-\frac{d^{2} \phi}{d x^{2}}=-\lambda\left(\phi^{2}-v^{2}\right) \phi \] 会有一个孤子解 \[ \phi(x)=v \tanh \left[\frac{m}{\sqrt{2}}\left(x-x_{0}\right)\right] \] 其中 \(x_{0}\) 可以看作孤子的位置，数值画图，取 \(x_{0}=0\)

能量密度为 \[ \begin{aligned} \epsilon &=\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial\left(\partial_{0} \phi\right)} \partial_{0} \phi-\mathcal{L} \\ &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{d \phi}{d t}\right)^{2}+\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{d \phi}{d x}\right)^{2}+\frac{\lambda}{4}\left(\phi^{2}-v^{2}\right)^{2} \\ &=\frac{m^{4}}{2 \lambda} \operatorname{sech}^{4}\left[\frac{m}{\sqrt{2}}\left(x-x_{0}\right)\right] \end{aligned} \] 数值画图，能量的局域化（localized）暗示孤子可以被看作一种粒子。

接下来积分求出总能量 \[ E=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \epsilon d x=\frac{2 \sqrt{2}}{3} \frac{m^{3}}{\lambda} \] 对这个解作Lorentz变换 \[ \phi(x, t)=v \tanh \left[\frac{m}{\sqrt{2}} \frac{\left(x-u t-x_{0}\right)}{\sqrt{1-u^{2}}}\right] \] 重新算一下总能量，发现任意时刻都有 \[ E^{\prime}=\frac{E}{\sqrt{1-u^{2}}} \] 那么这个解就可以被看作是具有能量 \(E\)，以速度 \(u\) 运动的粒子。很重要的是，随着时间推移，场和它的能量密度的构型仅仅发生了中心的平移，并没有发生耗散，确实就像是粒子。它像是不受任何阻碍地在运动，因而称为孤子。

KdV方程

我们自然会好奇孤子为什么能量不发散？一般来说色散效应的存在，波包在传播过程中会耗散。是什么克服了色散效应？

1895 年，荷兰科学家D. J. Kortweg和G. de Vries对孤立波给予了合理的解释。他们认为是非线性作用刚好平衡了水波的发散。以非线性建立了该孤立波的运动方程，KdV方程。 \[ \frac{\partial \phi}{\partial t}-6 \frac{\partial \phi}{\partial x}+ \frac{\partial^{3} \phi}{\partial x^{3}}=0 \] KdV 方程有多种孤波解

  • 亮孤子解（bright soliton） \[ \phi(x, t)=\frac{1}{2} c \operatorname{sech}^{2}\left[\frac{\sqrt{c}}{2}(x-c t-a)\right] \]

  • 暗孤子解（dark soliton）

\[ \phi(x, t)=a+b \tanh^{2} (1+c x+d t) \]

为什么这样取名？画出函数图像可以看出，第一个函数（bright soliton）的图相对于背景是一个凸出来的波包，第二个函数（dark soliton）的图相对于背景是一个凹下去的波包。

冷原子中的孤子

考虑存在相互作用的冷原子BEC \[ \hat{H}=\int d r \hat{\Psi}^{\dagger} \hat{H}_{0} \hat{\Psi}( r , t)+\frac{1}{2} \int d r d r ^{\prime} \hat{\Psi}^{\dagger}( r , t) \hat{\Psi}^{\dagger}\left( r ^{\prime}, t\right) V\left( r - r ^{\prime}\right) \hat{\Psi}\left( r ^{\prime}, t\right) \hat{\Psi}( r , t) \] 其中 \(V\left( r - r ^{\prime}\right)\) 是原子间的相互作用势，考虑稀薄冷原子气体的低能散射，那么 可以认为相互作用具有s波散长a，那么我们可以用一个有效相互作用势近似 \[ V\left(r^{\prime}-r\right)=g \delta\left(r^{\prime}-r\right) \] 其中

  • \(y=4 \pi \hbar^{2} a / m\)

GP方程

利用海森堡运动方程 \[ \begin{aligned} i \hbar\frac{\partial \hat{\Psi}}{\partial t}&=[\hat{\Psi}, \hat{H}]\\ &=\left[-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m} \nabla^{2}+V_{e s t}(r)+g|\Psi( r , t)|^{2}\right] \Psi( r , t) \end{aligned} \] 这就是Gross-Pitaevskii方程，简称GP方程

其中 \(V_{\text {est}}(r)\) 是外场，通常是一个谐势阱 \(\frac{1}{2} m \omega^{2} \vec{r}^{2}\) 。

考虑静态GP 方程 \[ \left[-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m} \nabla^{2}+V_{e s t}( r )+g\left|\Psi_{0}( r )\right|^{2}\right] \Psi_{0}( r )=\mu \Psi_{0}( r ) \] 其中

  • \(\Psi( r , t)=\Psi_{0}( r ) \exp (-i \mu t / \hbar)\)
  • \(\mu=\partial E / \partial N\)

可以看到GP方程是个非线性偏微分方程，该方程同样具有孤子解。简单起 见，我们考虑准一维的情况。 \[ i \partial_{t} \psi(z, t)=\left[-\frac{1}{2} \partial_{z}^{2}+V(z)+|\psi(z, t)|^{2}\right] \psi(z, t) \]

非线性薛定谔方程

因为 \(V(z)\) 非常小可以忽略，上式就变成非线性薛定讲方程(nonlinear Schrödinger equation) \[ i \partial_{t} \psi(z, t)=\left[-\frac{1}{2} \partial_{z}^{2}+|\psi(z, t)|^{2}\right] \psi(z, t) \] 有孤子解 \[ \psi(z, t)=\sqrt{n_{0}}(B \tanh \xi+i A) \exp \left[i\left(k z-\omega t+\theta_{0}\right)\right] \] 其中

  • \(\vec{\xi}=\sqrt{n_{0}} B\left[z-z_{0}(t)\right]\)
  • \(z_{0}(t)=v t+z_{0}\) 是孤子的中心位置
  • \(v=A \sqrt{n_{0}}+k\) 是孤子相对于背景原子的速度
  • \(\omega=1 / 2 k^{2}+n_{0}\) 是背景平面波的频率
  • \(n_{0}\)是原子数密度

相位

因为\(A\) 和 \(B\) 具有约束关系 \(A^{2}+B^{2}=1\)，所以可以令 \(A=\sin \phi\) 以及 \(B=\cos \phi\)，其中 $ $ 具有孤子相位角的含义。

由\(\tanh\) 的性质可知 \(\psi(z, t)=\sqrt{n_{0}}(B \tanh \xi+i A) \exp \left[i\left(k z-\omega t+\theta_{0}\right)\right]\) 在 \(z \rightarrow \pm \infty\) 时，在正负无穷两端分别具有不同的相位

所以 在孤子处波 函数必定存在相位跳变 \[ \Delta \phi=2\left[\tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{B}{A}\right)-\frac{\pi}{2}\right]=-2 \tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{A}{B}\right) \] 实验中可以利用孤子会导致波函数相位跳变的性质 在冷原子气体中产生孤子。

孤子的数密度 \(|\psi|^{2}\) 可以用相位角表示 \[ |\psi|^{2}=n_{0}\left(1-\cos ^{2} \phi \operatorname{sech}^{2} \xi\right) \] 如果 \(0 <\phi<\pi / 2\) ，则 \(|\psi|^{2}\) 函数值小于背景 \(n_{0}\) ，则为暗孤子（dark soliton）

PPT check

repulsive interaction, \(g>0\), \[ \begin{aligned} i \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)&=-\frac{1}{2} \partial_{x}^{2} \psi(x, t)+g|\psi(x, t)|^{2} \psi(x, t)-\mu \psi(x, t)\\ &=-\frac{1}{2} \partial_{x}^{2} \psi(x, t)+\left[g|\psi(x, t)|^{2}-\mu\right] \psi(x, t) \end{aligned} \] where

  • chemical potential is to ensure particle density is kept constant, \(\mu=g|\psi(x \rightarrow \pm \infty, t)|^{2}=g \rho_{0}\)

The general solution is \[ \begin{aligned} \psi(z)=\sqrt{\rho(z)} e^{i \phi(z)} \end{aligned} \] where

  • \(\rho(z)=\rho_{0}\left[1-\gamma^{2} \operatorname{sech}^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right]\) is density
  • \(\phi(z)=\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{v / c \gamma}{\tanh (\gamma z / \sqrt{2})}\right]\) is phase function

present in real part and image part \[ \begin{aligned} \psi(z)=\sqrt{\rho(z)} e^{i \phi(z)}=\sqrt{\rho_{0}}\left[\gamma \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+i \frac{v}{c}\right] \end{aligned} \] where

  • \(z=(x-v t) / \xi\)
  • with \(\xi \equiv \hbar / \sqrt{2 m g \rho_{0}}\) as a healing length.
  • \(c=\sqrt{g \rho_{0} / m}\) is the sound velocity for a wave in the condensate.
  • \(\gamma=\sqrt{1-v^{2} / c^{2}}\)

image part

After calculation (in Appendix), \(\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)\) is real, then \(i \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)\) is image. Therefore, the real part of right side of equation should be 0, the real part of right side of equation should be equal to left.

  • left

\[ i\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)=-i\sqrt{\rho_{0}}\gamma[1-\tanh^{2} \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)]\frac{v\gamma }{\sqrt{2}\xi} \]

the image part of \(\operatorname{Im}\left[i \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)\right]\) is \[ -\sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma\left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right] \frac{v \gamma}{\sqrt{2} \xi} \]

  • right

since \(\partial_{x}^{2} \psi(x, t)\) is real, the only image is in \(\left[g|\psi(x, t)|^{2}-\mu\right] \psi(x, t)\) term, then \(\operatorname{Im}\left[ \psi(x, t)\right]\) \[ \begin{aligned} \operatorname{Im}\left[ \psi(x, t)\right]&= \sqrt{\rho_{0}}\frac{v}{c} \end{aligned} \] then \[ \begin{aligned} g|\psi(x, t)|^{2}\operatorname{Im}\left[ \psi(x, t)\right]&=\left( g \rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right] -\mu\right)\sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ &=\left( g \rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right] -g \rho_{0}\right)\sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ &= g \rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}-1\right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ &= g \rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) -\gamma^{2}\right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ &= g \rho_{0}\gamma^{2} \left[\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) -1\right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ &= - g \rho_{0}\gamma^{2} \left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) \right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ \end{aligned} \]

  • left=right

\[ \begin{aligned} -\sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma\left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right] \frac{v \gamma}{\sqrt{2} \xi}&= -g \rho_{0} \gamma^{2}\left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma \frac{v \gamma}{\sqrt{2} \xi}&= g \rho_{0} \gamma^{2} \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{v}{c}\\ \frac{1 }{\sqrt{2} \xi}&= g \rho_{0} \frac{1}{c}\\ \frac{\sqrt{ m g \rho_{0}} }{\hbar}&= g \rho_{0} \frac{1}{c}\\ \frac{\sqrt{ m g \rho_{0}} }{\hbar}&= g \rho_{0} \sqrt{ \frac{m}{g \rho_{0}}}\\ \hbar&= 1 \end{aligned} \]

in many reference, people set \(\hbar=1\)

Real part

  • the first term

\[ \begin{aligned} -\frac{1}{2} \partial_{x}^{2} \psi(x, t) &= \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{\gamma^{3}}{ \xi^{2}} \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right]\\ \end{aligned} \]

  • the second term

\[ \begin{aligned} \operatorname{Re}\left[ \psi(x, t)\right]&= \sqrt{\rho_{0}}\gamma \end{aligned} \]

then \[ \begin{aligned} g|\psi(x, t)|^{2}\operatorname{Im}\left[ \psi(x, t)\right]&=\left( g \rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right] -\mu\right)\sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma\\ &= - g \rho_{0}\gamma^{2} \left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) \right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma \end{aligned} \]

  • the first term+the second term=0

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{\gamma^{3}}{ \xi^{2}} \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)\right]&= g \rho_{0}\gamma^{2} \left[1-\tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) \right] \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma\\ \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \frac{\gamma^{3}}{ \xi^{2}} \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) &= g \rho_{0}\gamma^{2} \sqrt{\rho_{0}} \gamma\\ \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{ \xi^{2}} \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right) &= g \rho_{0} \\ \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{ \xi^{2}} &= g \rho_{0} \\ \frac{1}{2} \frac{2 m g \rho_{0}}{ \hbar^{2}} &= g \rho_{0} \\ \frac{ m }{ \hbar^{2}} &= 1 \\ \end{aligned} \]

where we use \(\lim_{z \rightarrow + \infty}\tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)=1\)

Appendix

1. \(\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)\)

\[ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t) \]

  • \(\frac{d}{dx}\tanh (x)=1-\operatorname{tanh}(x)^{2}\)
  • $=-v/$

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{d}{dt}\tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)&=[1-\tanh^{2} \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)]\frac{\gamma }{\sqrt{2}} \frac{dz}{dt}\\ &=-[1-\tanh^{2} \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)]\frac{v\gamma }{\sqrt{2}\xi} \\ \end{aligned} \]

\[ i\frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(x, t)=-i\sqrt{\rho_{0}}\gamma[1-\tanh^{2} \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)]\frac{v\gamma }{\sqrt{2}\xi} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} |\psi(x, t)|^{2}&=\rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh^{2} \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+ \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right]\\ \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} g|\psi(x, t)|^{2} \psi(x, t)&=g\rho_{0}\left[\gamma^{2} \tanh ^{2}\left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}\right]\sqrt{\rho_{0}}\left[\gamma \tanh \left(\frac{\gamma z}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+i \frac{v}{c}\right]\\ \end{aligned} \]
