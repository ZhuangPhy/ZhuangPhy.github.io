1 Beyond Zero Bias Peaks of Majoranas

报告人 (Speaker)：邱靖凯

Found Majorana zero bias peaks and quantized conductances

What is next?

Confirm fundamental properties of Majoranas Before Majorana braiding

  1. Majorana hybridization: Energy splitting as a function of the distance between two Majorana modes
  2. Majorana spin texture

1.1 Majorana hybridization

Majorana oscillation \[ \Delta E_{ splitt } \propto \frac{\cos \left(k_{F} R\right)}{\sqrt{R}} e^{-R / \xi} \] where

  • \(\xi\): coherence length. (\(13.9 nm\) colierence length)
  • \(R\): intervortex distance

Second Evidence of Majoranas

  1. The length of the energy splitting oscillation \(\left(\pi / k_{F}=13.6 nm \right)\) is consistent with the Fermi momentum \(k_{F}\) of the Dirac cone.
  2. The length of the exponential decay \((\nu / \Delta=13.9 nm ),\) where \(\nu\) is the Fermi velocity of \(\quad\) surface Dirac cone and \(\Delta\) is the superconductor gap.

How to control Majorana distance?

The only controllable parameter is magnetic field strength (B).

  • Intervortex distance \(\quad R \propto 1 / \sqrt{B}\)
  • More than two vortices
  • 模拟 vortices 晶格
  • 能量分裂很小，精确度很差所以测量不出来，两个peak看起来还是像一个zero bias peak

需要一个完美的三角晶格

  1. However, the distribution of vortices is not perfect triangular lattice
  2. 大磁场下 vortices 的分布比较乱，不是完美三角晶格
  3. 不是完美三角晶格就看不到振荡随着距离变化而变化

Perfect triangular vortex lattice in Heterostructure of FeTe \(_{0.55} Se _{0.45}\) and \(NbSe _{2}\)

  1. 把贾金锋组提出的结构中的拓扑绝缘体\(Bi _{2} Te _{3}\) thin film换成 FeTe \(_{0.55} Se _{0.45}\) thin film
  2. from \(NbSe _{2}\) inherits perfect triangular lattice from \(NbSe _{2}\)
  3. The thickness of FeTe \(_{0.55} Se _{0.45}\) is greater than \(10 nm ,\) which is enough to kill the Majorana hybridization on the top and bottom surfaces

实验问题

  1. 现实的超导是比较“脏”的
  2. 超导的gap不均匀，Chemical composition不均匀
  3. 但是这会影响Local density of state和zero bias peaks吗？
  4. 实验证实： zero bias peaks No correlation with disorders 只有位置关系才影响
  5. 模拟证实：Inhomogeneous superconducting gap \(\Delta\)，Same LDOS in each vortex as long as Perfect triangular vortex lattice

小结

  • 观察到振荡 \(\Delta E_{\text {splitt}} \propto \frac{\cos \left(k_{F} R\right)}{\sqrt{R}} e^{-R / \xi}\)
  • 而且和Dirac corn 的费米动量\(k_{F}\)匹配

这就是Second Evidence of Majoranas

1.3 Majorana modes toward quantum computing

A big gap between the reality and Majorana quantum computing

DiViencenzo Criteria for Universal Quantum Computing

  1. Long decoherence time
  2. An ability to measure qubits one by one, without distributing the others.
  3. A universal set of quantum gates
  4. An ability to initialize the qubits
  5. A scalable physical system with well characterized qubits
1 Long decoherence time

Majorana decoherence time in the iron-based superconductors \[ \begin{array}{cll} \text {Majorana distance } & \text { Splitting Energy} & \text { Decoherence time } \\ \text { 50nm } & \text { 10 } \mu eV & T=0.7 n s \\ \text { 150nm } & \text { 7neV } & T=1 \mu \text { s } \\ \text { 250nm } & \text { 5peV } & T=1 ms \end{array} \] Exponential Majorana protection \(d \propto e^{-R / \lambda}\) 距离越远，寿命越长

  1. Exponential protection of zero modes in Majorana islands
2. An ability to measure qubits one by one, without distributing the others

Fuse two Majoranas and keep other Majoranas far away

3. A universal set of quantum gates 重要

Three essential quantum gates for universal quantum computing

  1. Hadamard
  2. T-gate
  3. CNOT

  • Majorana braiding leads to Hadamard gates and CNOT gates. These gates called Clifford gates have topological protection

  • Majorana braiding不能构造出T-gate

  • To realize T-gate, the topological protection must be scarified by fine tuning Majorana hybridization

4. An ability to initialize the qubits

With the combination of Hadamard-gate (H) and T-gate (T), We can get any single qubit state single qubit transformation \[ \left(\begin{array}{l} a \\ b \end{array}\right) \approx T^{n_{1}} H^{n_{1}} T^{n_{2}} H^{n_{2}} \cdots\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right) \]

5. A scalable physical system with well characterized qubits

The observation of multiple donut-shaped states \(\Rightarrow\) Scalable Majorana modes

However, scalable quantum gates are away from the reality

2 Majorana零模和具有拓扑保护的量子计算

报告人 (Speaker)：刘东 清华大学

报告摘要：Topological materials provide a protection from decoherence at the hardware level by using emergent non-Abelian anyons. The simplest non-Abelian anyon involves a defect that binds a Majorana zeroenergy mode. predicted to appear quite naturally in certain superconducting systems. I will review recent progress in the Majorana search, and the challenges in the validation of Majorana devices. Later I will introduce a simple measurement scheme to overcome the problem, and show robust, clear, and universal experimental signatures of Majorana zero modes. I will also discuss a serious type of errors in general topological quantum computation and Majorana qubit. Diabatic corrections only vanish as a power-law function with the length of time for the braid. This power-law behavior can wash out the advantages of topological quantum computation. We found that such diabatic errors can be detected and corrected by applying a sequence of parity measurements.

系统需要满足的条件

  1. Zero energy bound state
  2. Majorana operator \(\gamma=\gamma^{+}\)

Usually quasi-particles carry charge： Particle \(c^{\dagger} \neq c\) hole

所以我们需要一个电荷中性的系统——比如说超导

  1. \(\gamma_{E \uparrow}=\int d x\left[u_{E}(x) \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}(x)+v_{E}(x) \psi_{\downarrow}(x)\right]\) s-wave SC quasi-particles carry different spin
  2. It is impossible to have \(\gamma_{E}^{\dagger}=\gamma_{E}\)
  3. \(\bar{\gamma}_{E}=\int d x\left[u_{E}(x) \psi^{\dagger}(x)+v_{E}(x) \psi(x)\right]\) Unconventional spinless SC 去掉自旋
  4. Arrange \(u_{E}\) and \(v_{E} \Rightarrow \gamma_{E}^{\dagger}=\gamma_{E}\)
