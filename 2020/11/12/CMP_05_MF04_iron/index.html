1.3 Nearly quantized conductance plateau of MZMs

背景

  • Quantized ZPB conductance plateau in nanowires.
  • Quantized conductance plateau is the hallmark for Majorana.

那么在我们的体系中是否有quantized conductance plateau

  • Majorana symmetry leads to the resonant Andreev reflection.
  • Only MZMs hold quantized conductance plateau.

测量方式

  • 沿着中心往外（不随着空间移动和劈裂的零能峰）
  • 在中心，从高到底（在零能对应的有一段区间dI/dV保持了一段不变的quantized conductance ）
  • 在中心，从高到底（其他能量对应的dI/dV一直增长 ）

The ZPBs never split during the measurement. Zero-bias conductance curve shows a plateau value at \(0.64 G _{0}\). Other conductance curves keep increasing with \(G _{ N }\).

The conductance behaviors of trivial states

  • Finite-energy CBSs keep increasing and show no plateau.
  • Conductance curves at zero field show no plateau.

One case achieved quantized Others are not ?

1 Instrumental broadening

  • Instrumental broadening varies with the modulation voltage.
  • Instrumental broadening reduces \(G_{p}\) from quantized value.

2 Quasi-particle poisoning

  • Conductance plateau values vary form different vortices.
  • Conductance plateau values correlated with FWHM of ZPBs.
  • Quasiparticle poisoning reduces \(G_{p}\) from quantized value.

Quasi-particle poisoning 会导致峰降低，展宽变宽

Reversibility and reproducibility of plateau

  • Tip and sample states did not change during the measurements.

Summary

  1. Robust zero-bias peaks and conductance plateau are observed when increasing the tunnel-coupling between tip and the sample.
  2. CBSs and other trivial states show no plateau feature, which is protected by the Majorana particle-hole symmetry.
  3. Neatly quantized conductance plateaus are reversible and reproducible. Both the instrumental broadening and the quasiparticle poisoning effect reduce the plateau value from the quantized conductance.
