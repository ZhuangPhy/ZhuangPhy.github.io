  • 松原函数的运动方程
  • 海森堡算符的运动方程

弱相互作用超导体中的哈密顿量 \[ \begin{aligned} H=& \sum_{\alpha} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \psi_{\alpha}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x})\left(-\frac{1}{2 m} \nabla^{2}\right) \psi_{\alpha}(\boldsymbol{x}) \\ &+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha, \beta} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime} \psi_{\alpha}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}) \psi_{\beta}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) V\left(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) \psi_{\beta}\left(\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) \psi_{\alpha}(\boldsymbol{x}) \end{aligned} \]

松原函数法

1 二次量子化

把场算符在 单粒子完备集 上展开：

  • \(\psi_{\alpha}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x})=\sum_{k_{1} }a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{*}(\boldsymbol{x})\)
  • \(\psi_{\alpha}(\boldsymbol{x})\sum_{k_{1} }a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \alpha} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \alpha}(\boldsymbol{x})\)

这个基底上的系数 就是 产生湮没算符。

把哈密顿量用产生湮没算符来表示： \[ \begin{aligned} H=& \sum_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k}_{2} \alpha} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{*}(\boldsymbol{x})\left(-\frac{1}{2 m} \nabla^{2}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \alpha} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \alpha}(\boldsymbol{x}) \\ &+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k}_{2} \boldsymbol{k}_{3} \boldsymbol{k}_{4} \alpha, \beta} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{*}(\boldsymbol{x}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \beta}^{\dagger} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \beta}^{*}\left(\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) \\ & \cdot V\left(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{3} \beta} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{3} \beta}\left(\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{4} \alpha} \varphi_{\boldsymbol{k}_{4} \alpha}(\boldsymbol{x}) \end{aligned} \] 设单粒子完备集就是平面波 \(e^{i k \cdot x}\) , 我们把哈密顿量写成动量空间的形式(空间变量积分掉)。

  1. 第一项

\[ \begin{aligned} H_{0} &=\sum_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k}_{1} \cdot x}\left(-\frac{1}{2 m} \nabla^{2}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}} \\ &=\sum_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \frac{\boldsymbol{k}^{2}}{2 m} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \delta_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1}, \boldsymbol{k}_{ }} \\&=\sum_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \varepsilon_{\boldsymbol{k}} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \end{aligned} \]

其中我们符号代换 \(\boldsymbol{k}=\boldsymbol{k}_{1}\)，以及利用了积分技巧 \[ \begin{aligned} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k}_{1} \cdot x} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}} &= \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} (\boldsymbol{k} -\boldsymbol{k}_{1}) \cdot \boldsymbol{x} } \\ &= \delta_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1}, \boldsymbol{k}_{ }} \end{aligned} \]

  1. 第二项

\[ \begin{aligned} H_{1}&=\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\boldsymbol{p} \boldsymbol{k} \boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k}_{2} \boldsymbol{q} \alpha, \boldsymbol{\beta}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x} \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k}_{1} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \beta}^{\dagger} \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k}_{2} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{k} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{q} \cdot\left(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}\right)} \\& \cdot V(\boldsymbol{q}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{3} \beta} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{p} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{4} \alpha} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \boldsymbol{k} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}} \\ =& \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\boldsymbol{p} \boldsymbol{k} \boldsymbol{k}_{1} \boldsymbol{k}_{2} \boldsymbol{q} \alpha, \beta} V(\boldsymbol{q}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{k}_{2} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p} \beta} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \delta_{\boldsymbol{p}, \boldsymbol{k}_{2}+\boldsymbol{q}} \delta_{\boldsymbol{k}, \boldsymbol{k}_{1}-\boldsymbol{q}} \\ =& \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\boldsymbol{k} p \boldsymbol{q} \alpha, \boldsymbol{\beta}} V(\boldsymbol{q}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}+\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p} \beta} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \end{aligned} \]

其中要注意, \(\boldsymbol{q}=0\) 的项表示均匀电子气的相互排斥库仑作用。\(\boldsymbol{q}=0\) 和均匀正电荷背 景之间的相互吸引库仑作用正好相互抵消。因此，可以去掉 \(\boldsymbol{q}=0\) 的项。而 \(\boldsymbol{q} \neq 0\) 的项表亦电子气的非均匀部分，也就是涨落部分之间的相互作用. 这一作用 不但不为零, 而且有可能因为其他因素的影响而成为电子之间的吸引作用。动量空 间的哈密顿量是 \[ H=\sum_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \varepsilon_{\boldsymbol{k}} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\boldsymbol{q} \neq 0 \boldsymbol{k} p \alpha, \beta} V(\boldsymbol{q}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}+\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p} \beta} a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha} \]

2 南部格林函数

在超导相中, 可以出现同时湮没或者同时产生一对相反动量, 相反自旋的电子。 为此, 物理学及南部 (Nambu) 定义了以下的二分量波函数： \[ \Psi(\boldsymbol{x}, t)=\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}(\boldsymbol{x}, t) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}, t) \end{array}\right), \quad \Psi^{\dagger}(t)=\left(\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}, t), \psi_{\downarrow}(\boldsymbol{x}, t)\right) \] 注意与一般的二分量波函数的区别 \[ \Psi(\boldsymbol{x}, t)=\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}(\boldsymbol{x}, t) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}(\boldsymbol{x}, t) \end{array}\right), \Psi^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}, t)=\left(\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}, t), \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x}, t)\right) \] 在动量空间中, 南部波函数为 \[ \begin{aligned} G_{\mathrm{N}}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) &=\left\langle\left\langle\Psi\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \Psi^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &=\left\langle\left\langle\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) \end{array}\right) |\left(\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right), \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \end{array}\right)\\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} G_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) & F\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) \\ F^{+}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) & -G_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}, \boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \] 非对角元就是 反常格林函数 \(F\left(x_{1} t_{1}, x_{2} t_{2}\right)\) 。 \(F^{+}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\) 表示产生一对电子，\(F\left(x_{1} t_{1}, x_{2} t_{2}\right)\) 表示湮没一对电子的过程.

2.1 推迟和超前格林函数

相应地, 对角元 对应 推迟和超前格林函数：

  • 推迟格林函数

\[ \begin{aligned} G^{\mathrm{R}}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) &=\left\langle\left\langle\Psi\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \Psi^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{R}}=-\mathrm{i} \theta\left(t_{1}-t_{2}\right)\left\langle\left\{\Psi\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right), \Psi^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\}\right\rangle \\ &=-\mathrm{i} \theta\left(t_{1}-t_{2}\right)\left\langle\left\{\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) \end{array}\right),\left(\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right), \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right)\right\}\right\rangle \\ &=-\mathrm{i} \theta\left(t_{1}-t_{2}\right)\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\{\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right), \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\}\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\{\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right), \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\}\right\rangle \\ \left\langle\left\{\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right), \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\}\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\{\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right), \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\}\right\rangle \end{array}\right) \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{R}} & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{R}} \\ \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{R}} & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{R}} \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

  • 超前格林函数

\[ \begin{aligned} G^{A}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right) &=\left\langle\left\langle\Psi\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \Psi^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{A}} \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{A}} & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{A}} \\ \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{A}} & \left\langle\left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right) | \psi_{\downarrow}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle^{\mathrm{A}} \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

推迟和超前格林函数的关系 \[ \left\{\left[G\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}\right)\right]^{\mathrm{A}}\right\}^{+}=\left[G\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{2} t_{2}, \boldsymbol{x}_{1} t_{1}\right)\right]^{\mathrm{R}} \]

2.2 小于格林函数

\[ \begin{aligned} G^{<}\left( x _{1} t_{1}, x _{2} t_{2}\right) &= i \left\langle\left\langle\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \end{array}\right)\left(\psi_{\uparrow}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right), \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right)\right\rangle\right.\\ &= i \left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \psi_{\uparrow}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right\rangle & \left\langle\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right\rangle \\ \left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \psi_{\uparrow}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right\rangle & \left\langle\psi_{\downarrow}\left( x _{2} t_{2}\right) \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}\left( x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right\rangle \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

性质:

  • \(\left[G^{<}\left( x _{1} t_{1}, x _{2} t_{2}\right)\right]^{+}=-\left[G\left( x _{2} t_{2}, x _{1} t_{1}\right)\right]^{<}\)
  • 设\(G^{<}\)是时间差的函数, 其傅里叶变换满足如下关系:\(G^{<}(\omega)=-\left[G^{<}(\omega)\right]^{+}\)

在动量空间中 \[ \begin{aligned} G_{ N p }\left(t_{1}, t_{2}\right) &=\left\langle\left\langle\Psi_{ p }\left(t_{1}\right) \mid \Psi_{ p }^{\dagger}\left(t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &=\left\langle\left\langle\left(\begin{array}{c} a_{ p \uparrow}\left(t_{1}\right) \\ a_{- p \downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{1}\right) \end{array}\right) \mid\left(a_{ p \uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{2}\right), a_{- p \downarrow}\left(t_{2}\right)\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\langle a_{ p \uparrow}\left(t_{1}\right) \mid a_{ p \uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle a_{ p \uparrow}\left(t_{1}\right) \mid a_{- p \downarrow}\left(t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ \left\langle\left\langle a_{- p \downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{1}\right) \mid a_{ p \uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle a_{- p \downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(t_{1}\right) \mid a_{- p \downarrow}\left(t_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \end{array}\right) \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{c} G_{ p \uparrow}\left(t_{1}, t_{2}\right) &F_{ p }\left(t_{1}, t_{2}\right)\\ F_{ p }^{+}\left(t_{1}, t_{2}\right) & -G_{- p \downarrow}\left(t_{2}, t_{1}\right) \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

2.3南部松原函数

南部表象下的虚时二分量波函数，只要把原来的实时间 \(t\) 换成虚时间 \(\tau\) 即可. 在 空间表象和动量表象中分别如下： \[ \begin{array}{l} \Psi(\boldsymbol{x} \tau)=\left(\begin{array}{c} \psi_{\uparrow}(\boldsymbol{x} \tau) \\ \psi_{\downarrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x} \tau) \end{array}\right), \quad \Psi^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x} \tau)=\left(\psi_{\uparrow}^{\dagger}(\boldsymbol{x} \tau), \psi_{\downarrow}(\boldsymbol{x} \tau)\right) \\ \Psi_{\boldsymbol{p}}(\tau)=\left(\begin{array}{c} a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}(\tau) \\ a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}^{\dagger}(\tau) \end{array}\right), \quad \Psi_{\boldsymbol{p}}^{\dagger}(\tau)=\left(a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}^{\dagger}(\tau), a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}(\tau)\right) \end{array} \] 动量空间的松原函数是 \[ \begin{aligned} G_{\boldsymbol{p}}\left(\tau_{1}, \tau_{2}\right) &=-\left\langle\left\langle\Psi_{\boldsymbol{p}}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | \Psi_{\boldsymbol{p}}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &=-\left(\begin{array}{cc} \left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ \left\langle\left\langle a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle & \left\langle\left\langle a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \end{array}\right) \\ &=\left(\begin{array}{cc} G_{\boldsymbol{p} \uparrow}\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right) & F_{\boldsymbol{p}}\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right) \\ F_{\boldsymbol{p}}^{+}\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right) & -G_{-\boldsymbol{p} \downarrow}\left(\tau_{2}-\tau_{1}\right) \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

定义了这么多格林函数，他们之间是什么关系

原则上, 用热力学格林函数和松原函数处理具体的问题都可以。

4 南部松原函数的运动方程

巨正则系综的形式：\(K=H-\mu N\)。算符的运动方程 \[ \begin{aligned} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau} a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}(\tau) &=\mathrm{e}^{\tau K / \hbar}\left[K, a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}\right] \mathrm{e}^{-\tau K / \hbar} \\ &=-\left(\varepsilon_{\boldsymbol{p}}-\mu\right) a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}(\tau)-\sum_{\boldsymbol{q} \neq 0 \boldsymbol{k} \alpha} V(\boldsymbol{q}) a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger}(\tau) a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}(\tau) a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}(\tau) \end{aligned} \] 同理 \[ \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau} a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}(\tau)=\left(\varepsilon_{p}-\mu\right) a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}(\tau)-\sum_{q \neq 0 k \alpha} V(q) a_{p+q \sigma}^{\dagger}(\tau) a_{k-q \alpha}^{\dagger}(\tau) a_{k \alpha}(\tau) \]

4.1 松原函数的运动方程

\[ \begin{aligned} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{1}} G_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}, \tau_{2}\right)=&-\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau}\left[\theta\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right)\left\langle a_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right.\\ &\left.-\theta\left(\tau_{2}-\tau_{1}\right)\left\langle a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right) a_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right)\right\rangle\right] \\ =&-\hbar \delta\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right)-\left\langle\left\langle\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{1}} a_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ =&-\hbar \delta\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}\right)-\left(\varepsilon_{p}-\mu\right)\left\langle\left\langle a_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &+\sum_{q \neq 0 k \alpha} V(q)\left\langle\left\langle a_{k-q \alpha}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) a_{k \alpha}\left(\tau_{1}\right) a_{p-q \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \end{aligned} \]

最后一项做分解近似，排除 \(\boldsymbol{q}=0\) 之后, 有以下几种情况： \[ \begin{aligned} &\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}\left(\tau_{1}\right) a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ =&\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}^{+}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{p \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ &+\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}\left(\tau_{1}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \end{aligned} \]

  • 第一项中, 第一个因子 \(\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\right\rangle\) 只有在 \(k=p, \alpha=\sigma\) 时才不为零。
  • 第一项中, 第二个因子 \(G_{p \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}, \tau_{2}\right)\) 是正常格林函数
  • 第二项中，是两个反常格林函数，但必须是 \(\boldsymbol{k}=-\boldsymbol{p}+\boldsymbol{q}, \alpha=-\sigma\) 分别是

\[ \begin{aligned} &\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha^{\dagger}}\left(\tau_{1}^{+}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}} \alpha\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ =& \delta_{\boldsymbol{p}, \boldsymbol{k}} \delta_{\alpha \sigma} n_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma} G_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}, \tau_{2}\right) \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} &\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q} \sigma}\left(\tau_{1}^{+}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{k} \alpha}\left(\tau_{1}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle\left\langle\left\langle a_{\boldsymbol{k}-\boldsymbol{q} \alpha}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{1}\right) | a_{\boldsymbol{p} \sigma}^{\dagger}\left(\tau_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right\rangle \\ =& \delta_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q},-k} \delta_{\boldsymbol{\alpha},-\sigma} F_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q}}(0) F_{\boldsymbol{p}}^{+}\left(\tau_{1}, \tau_{2}\right) \end{aligned} \]

6 一些物理量的计算

能隙函数的自洽方程

激发一个电子的能量至少是 \(\Delta\). 因此在激发态和基态之间有一能隙. 但注意这 一能隙并不是\(\Delta\). 由于超导基态中电子是配对的, 电子只能成对地湮没或者产生. 因 而不能只激发一个电子. 必须在超导态中同时湮没两个电子, 把它们送到激发态上。 假定到达激发态后这两个电子的能量是 \(E\left(p_{1}\right)\) 和 \(E\left(p_{2}\right)\), 那么: \(E\left(p_{1}\right)+E\left(p_{2}\right)>2 \Delta\) 宝少需要 \(2\Delta\)才能实现电子的激发。因此\(2\Delta\)才是超导态的真正的能隙。

假设库仑相互作用的傅里叶分量是常量： \[ V(\boldsymbol{p})=\lambda \] 把反常松原函数代入能隙函数的定义 \[ \begin{aligned} \Delta &=-\lambda \sum_{\boldsymbol{q} \neq 0} F_{\boldsymbol{p}-\boldsymbol{q}}\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}=0^{+}\right)=-\lambda \sum_{\boldsymbol{q}} F_{\boldsymbol{q}}\left(\tau_{1}-\tau_{2}=0^{+}\right) \\ &=\frac{\lambda}{\beta} \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \sum_{n} F\left(\boldsymbol{p}, \mathrm{i} \omega_{n}\right) \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \omega_{n} 0^{+}} \\ &=\frac{\lambda}{\beta} \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \sum_{n} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \omega_{n} 0^{+}} \frac{\Delta}{\hbar^{2} \omega_{n}^{2}+E_{\boldsymbol{p}}^{2}} \end{aligned} \]

因为我们假设了\(\Delta\) 是常量，与动量积分 \(\int d p\) 无关，可以提出来，与左边 \(\Delta\) 消除 \[ \begin{aligned} 1 &=\frac{\lambda}{\beta} \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \sum_{n} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \omega_{n} 0^{+}} \frac{1}{2 E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}\left(\frac{1}{\mathrm{i} \omega_{n}-E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}-\frac{1}{\mathrm{i} \omega_{n}+E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}\right) \\ &=-\frac{\lambda}{2(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \frac{1}{E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}\left(\frac{1}{\mathrm{e}^{\beta E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}+1}-\frac{1}{\mathrm{e}^{-\beta E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}+1}\right) \\ &=\frac{\lambda}{2} \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \frac{1}{E_{\boldsymbol{p}}} \tanh \frac{\beta E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}{2} \end{aligned} \] 在第二个等号计算我们用了公式 \(\frac{1}{\beta \hbar} \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} F\left(\mathrm{i} \omega_{n}\right)=-\frac{1}{2 \pi \mathrm{i}} \int_{\mathrm{C}} \frac{F(z)}{\mathrm{e}^{\beta \hbar z}+1} \mathrm{d} z\) 求和的是费米子频率 \(\omega_{n}=(2 n+1) \pi / \beta\)

现在把对动量的积分换成对能量的积分 \[ \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \rightarrow \int N(\xi) \mathrm{d} \xi=N(0) \int \mathrm{d} \xi \] 这儿把态密度用化学势处的态密度来近似. 进一步, 近似成零温时的化学考, 也就是费米能处的态密度. 费米能处的态密度满足 \(N(0)=\frac{N k_{\mathrm{F}}}{2 \pi^{2}}\)

一能量是以化学势为零点的, 所以能量可正可负. 把能量的积分上限设为有限值 \(\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}\) \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\lambda}{2} \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{3}} \int \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{p} \frac{1}{E_{\boldsymbol{p}}} \tanh \frac{\beta E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}{2} &=1 \\ \lambda N(0) \int_{0}^{\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}} \mathrm{d} \xi \frac{1}{E_{\boldsymbol{p}}} \tanh \frac{\beta E_{\boldsymbol{p}}}{2}&=1 \\ \lambda N(0) \int_{0}^{\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}} \mathrm{d} \xi \frac{1}{\sqrt{\xi^{2}+\Delta(T)^{2}}} \tanh \frac{\beta \sqrt{\xi^{2}+\Delta(T)^{2}}}{2}&=1 \end{aligned} \]

零温时的能隙

\(T=0\) 时，\(\tanh (\beta \sqrt{\xi^{2}+\Delta^{2}(0)} / 2)=1\) \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{\lambda N(0)} &=\int_{0}^{\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}} \mathrm{d} \xi \frac{1}{\sqrt{\xi^{2}+\Delta^{2}(0)}}=\ln |[\xi+\sqrt{\xi^{2}+\Delta^{2}(0)}]|_{0}^{\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}} \\ &=\ln \frac{\hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}+\sqrt{\hbar^{2} \omega_{\mathrm{D}}^{2}+\Delta^{2}(0)}}{\Delta(0)} \approx \ln \frac{2 \hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}}{\Delta(0)} \end{aligned} \] 我们假设了能量上限远大于 \(\Delta(0)\) \[ \omega_{\mathrm{D}} \gg \Delta(0) \] 此式称为弱耦合极限。由等式 \(\frac{1}{\lambda N(0)} = \ln \frac{2 \hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}}}{\Delta(0)}\) 可以得到零温时的能隙 \[ E_{\mathrm{g}}=2 \Delta(0)=4 \hbar \omega_{\mathrm{D}} \mathrm{e}^{-1 / \lambda N(0)} \] 这个量随着相互作用强度减弱或者费米能处的态密度的减弱而迅速超于零。

做傅里叶变换 \[ \begin{aligned} H_{1} &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{k_{1} k_{2} k_{3} k_{4} \alpha, \beta} \int d x a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} e ^{- i k _{1} \cdot x } a_{ k _{2} \beta}^{\dagger} e ^{- i k _{2} \cdot x } a_{ k _{3} \beta} e ^{ i k _{3} \cdot x } a_{ k _{4} \alpha} e ^{ i k _{4} \cdot x } \\ &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k _{2} k _{3} k _{4} \alpha, \beta} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{ k _{2} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{ k _{3} \beta} a_{ k _{4} \alpha} \delta_{ k _{1}+ k _{2}, k _{3}+ k _{4}} \\ &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k _{3} k _{4} \alpha, \beta} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{ k _{3}+ k _{4}- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{ k _{3} \beta} a_{ k _{4} \alpha} \end{aligned} \] 再取 \(k_{3}=-k_{4},\) 同样得到 \[ \begin{aligned} H_{1} =-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k \alpha, \beta} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha} \end{aligned} \]

能隙

平均场近似: \[ \begin{aligned} H_{1} &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k \alpha, \beta} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger} a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha}\\ &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k \alpha, \beta}\left(\left\langle a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger}\right\rangle a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha}+a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger}\left\langle a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha}\right\rangle\right) \end{aligned} \] 只能同时潭没或者产生一对自旋相反的粒子. \[ \begin{aligned} H_{1} &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k _{1} k \alpha, \beta}\left(\left\langle a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger}\right\rangle a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha}+a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1} \beta}^{\dagger}\left\langle a_{- k \beta} a_{ k \alpha}\right\rangle\right) \\&=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k k _{1} \alpha}\left(\left\langle a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1}-\alpha}^{\dagger}\right\rangle a_{- k -\alpha} a_{ k \alpha}+a_{ k \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k -\alpha}^{\dagger}\left\langle a_{- k _{1}-\alpha} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}\right\rangle\right) \end{aligned} \] 能隙函数的定义 \[ \lambda\left\langle a_{-k \downarrow} a_{k \uparrow}\right\rangle=-\Delta(k) e ^{- i \phi} \] 平均场哈密顿量 \[ \begin{aligned} H_{1} &=-\frac{\lambda}{2} \sum_{ k k _{1} \alpha}\left(\left\langle a_{ k _{1} \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k _{1}-\alpha}^{\dagger}\right\rangle a_{- k -\alpha} a_{ k \alpha}+a_{ k \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k -\alpha}^{\dagger}\left\langle a_{- k _{1}-\alpha} a_{ k _{1} \alpha}\right\rangle\right)\\ &=\sum_{k k_{1}}\left(a_{-k \downarrow} a_{k \uparrow} \Delta\left(k_{1}\right) e ^{ i \phi}+a_{k \uparrow}^{\dagger} a_{-k \downarrow}^{\dagger} \Delta\left(k_{1}\right) e ^{- i \phi}\right) \end{aligned} \] 对 \(k _{1}\) 求和 \(\Delta=\sum_{k_{1}} \Delta\left( k _{1}\right)\)

由于做了平均场近似现在的 是一个与动量无关的物理量. \[ \begin{aligned} K &=\sum_{ k \alpha} \xi_{ k \alpha} a_{ k \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{ k \alpha}+\frac{1}{2} \Delta \sum_{ k \alpha}\left(a_{ k \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{- k -\alpha}^{\dagger} e ^{- i \phi}+\text { h.c. }\right) \\ &=\sum_{ k \alpha} \xi_{ k \alpha} a_{ k \alpha}^{\dagger} a_{ k \alpha}+\Delta \sum_{ k }\left[e^{- i \phi} a_{ k \uparrow}^{\dagger} a_{- k \downarrow}^{\dagger}+ h.c. \right] \end{aligned} \]

海森堡算符的运动方程

先计算出海森伯算符的表达式, 然后用它们来组成格林函数.

海森伯算符 \[ \begin{aligned} i \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} a_{k \sigma}(t) &= e ^{ i K t / \hbar}\left[a_{k \sigma}, K\right] e ^{- i K t / \hbar} \\ &=\varepsilon_{k \sigma} a_{k \sigma}(t)+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{-k,-\sigma}^{\dagger}(t) \\ i \frac{\partial}{\partial t} a_{k \sigma}^{\dagger}(t) &=-\varepsilon_{k \sigma} a_{k \sigma}^{\dagger}(t)-\sigma \Delta e ^{ i \phi} a_{-k,-\sigma}(t) \end{aligned} \] 求导 \[ \begin{aligned} -\hbar^{2} \frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial t^{2}} a_{ k \sigma}(t)&=\xi_{ k \sigma}^{2} a_{ k \sigma}(t)+\Delta^{2} a_{ k , \sigma}(t)\\&=\left(\xi_{ k \sigma}^{2}+\Delta^{2}\right) a_{ k , \sigma}(t) \end{aligned} \] 其中

  • 用到 \(\xi_{-k-\sigma}=\xi_{k \sigma} .\)
  • 令 \(E_{ k \sigma}=\sqrt{\xi_{ k \sigma}^{2}+\Delta^{2}}\)

试探解

方程的解 \[ a_{ k \sigma }(t)=A_{ k \sigma} \exp \left( i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)+B_{ k \sigma} \exp \left(- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right) \] 初始条件

  • \(a_{k \sigma}=a_{k \sigma}(0)=A_{k \sigma}+B_{k \sigma}\)
  • \(a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger}=a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger}(0)=A_{- k -\sigma}^{*}+B_{- k -\sigma}^{*}\)

求导 \[ i \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} a_{ k \sigma}(t)=-E_{ k \sigma} A_{ k \sigma} \exp \left( i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)+E_{ k \sigma} B_{ k \sigma} \exp \left(- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right) \] 对比 \(i \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} a_{ k \sigma}(t)=\varepsilon_{ k \sigma} a_{ k \sigma}(t)+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k ,-\sigma}^{\dagger}(t)\) \[ \begin{aligned} & E_{ k \sigma}\left[-A_{ k \sigma} \exp \left( i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)+B_{ k \sigma} \exp \left(- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)\right] \\ =& \xi_{ k \sigma} a_{k \sigma}(t)+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k ,-\sigma}^{\dagger}(t) \\ =& \xi_{ k \sigma}\left[A_{ k \sigma} \exp \left( i E_{ k \sigma} t\right)+B_{ k \sigma} \exp \left(- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)\right] \\ &+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi}\left[A_{- k -\sigma}^{*} \exp \left(- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)+B_{- k -\sigma}^{*} \exp \left( i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar\right)\right] \end{aligned} \] 两边相同时间指数项的系数 \[ \begin{aligned} -E_{ k \sigma} A_{ k \sigma}&=\xi_{ k \sigma} A_{ k \sigma}+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} B_{- k -\sigma}^{*} \\ E_{ k \sigma} B_{ k \sigma}&=\xi_{ k \sigma} B_{ k \sigma}+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} A_{- k -\sigma}^{*} \end{aligned} \] 两式相加 \[ -E_{ k \sigma}\left(A_{ k \sigma}-B_{ k \sigma}\right)=\xi_{ k \sigma} a_{ k \sigma}+\sigma \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger} \] 结合 \(a_{k \sigma}=a_{k \sigma}(0)=A_{k \sigma}+B_{k \sigma}\) 解出四个系数 \[ \begin{aligned} A_{ k \sigma}&=\frac{1}{2}\left(1-\frac{\xi_{ k \sigma}}{E_{ k \sigma}}\right) a_{ k \sigma}-\frac{\sigma}{2 E_{ k \sigma}} \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger}\\ B_{k \sigma}&=\frac{1}{2}\left(1+\frac{\xi_{k \sigma}}{E_{k \sigma}}\right) a_{k \sigma}+\frac{\sigma}{2 E_{k \sigma}} \Delta e ^{- i \phi} a_{-k-\sigma}^{\dagger} \end{aligned} \] 定义以下的量

  • \(u_{k \sigma}^{2}=\frac{1}{2}\left(1+\frac{\xi_{k \sigma}}{E_{k \sigma}}\right)\)
  • \(v_{ k \sigma}^{2}=\frac{1}{2}\left(1-\frac{\xi_{ k \sigma}}{E_{ k \sigma}}\right)\)

得以下关系

  • \(v_{ k \sigma}^{2}+u_{ k \sigma}^{2}=1\)
  • \(u_{ k \sigma}^{2}-v_{ k \sigma}^{2}=\frac{\xi_{ k \sigma}}{E_{ k \sigma}}\)
  • \(2 u_{ k \sigma} v_{ k \sigma}=\left(1-\frac{\xi_{ k \sigma}^{2}}{E_{ k \sigma}^{2}}\right)^{1 / 2}=\frac{\Delta}{E_{ k \sigma}}\)
  • \(v_{ k \sigma}^{4}=v_{ k \sigma}^{2}-\frac{\Delta^{2}}{4 E_{ k \sigma}^{2}}\)
  • \(u_{k \sigma}^{4}=u_{k \sigma}^{2}-\frac{\Delta^{2}}{4 E_{k \sigma}^{2}}\)

最终解

解出海森伯算符的表达式 \[ \begin{aligned} a_{ k \sigma}(t)=& A_{ k \sigma} e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}+B_{ k \sigma} e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar} \\ =&\left(v_{ k \sigma}^{2} a_{ k \sigma}-\frac{\sigma \Delta}{2 E_{ k \sigma}} e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger}\right) e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar} \\ &+\left(u_{ k \sigma}^{2} a_{k \sigma}+\frac{\sigma \Delta}{2 E_{ k \sigma}} e ^{- i \phi} a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger}\right) e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar} \\ =&\left(v_{ k \sigma}^{2} e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}+u_{ k \sigma}^{2} e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}\right) a_{ k \sigma} \\ &+\frac{\sigma \Delta}{2 E_{ k \sigma}} e ^{- i \phi}\left(- e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}+ e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}\right) a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger} \end{aligned} \] 简记为 \[ a_{ k \sigma}(t)=D_{1} a_{ k \sigma}+\sigma D_{2} a_{- k -\sigma}^{\dagger} \]

  • \(D_{1}=v_{ k \sigma}^{2} e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}+u_{ k \sigma}^{2} e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}\)
  • \(D_{2}=\frac{\Delta}{2 E_{ k \sigma}} e ^{- i \phi}\left(- e ^{ i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}+ e ^{- i E_{ k \sigma} t / \hbar}\right)\)
