几何相位

对于系统：

  1. 绝热
  2. 缓变
  3. 回归到原本参数

\[ | n (T)\rangle \rightarrow e^{i \alpha}| n (0)\rangle=| m (T)\rangle \]

Schrödinger方程 \[ H(t)| n (t)\rangle=E_{n}| n (t)\rangle \] 一个周期 \(T\) 过后态矢就得到一整个相位 \[ \begin{array}{l} \alpha=\delta+\gamma \end{array} \] 其中动力学相位可以被规范变换吸收掉 \[ \delta=-\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{0}^{T} d t\langle m(t)|H(t)| m(t)\rangle \] 几何相位不可被规范变换吸收掉 \[ \gamma=i \int_{0}^{T} d t\left\langle n(t)\left|\frac{d}{d t}\right| n(t)\right\rangle \] 几何相位随着时间演化的运动方程为 \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{d \gamma_{n}}{d t}(t) &=i\left\langle n (t) \mid \frac{d n (t)}{d t}\right\rangle \\ &=i\left\langle n (t) \mid \nabla_{t} n (t)\right\rangle \end{aligned} \] 可以看到, 决定了 \(\gamma_{n}(T) \neq \gamma_{n}(0)\) 的就是右边的这个项 \[ \begin{aligned} \gamma_{n}(T)-\gamma_{n}(0)=\gamma_{n}(C) &=\int_{0}^{T} d t \dot{\gamma}_{n}(t) \\ &=i \int_{0}^{T} d t\left\langle n (t) \mid \nabla_{t} n (t)\right\rangle \\ &=i \oint d t\left\langle n (t) \mid \nabla_{t} n (t)\right\rangle \end{aligned} \] 于是利用Stokes公式 \[ \begin{aligned} \gamma_{n}(C) &=i \oint_{C} d t A _{n}(t) \\ &=\iint_{S} d S \nabla \times A _{n}(t) \end{aligned} \] #### 电磁场类比

于是类比于电磁场，

这个矢势 \[ A _{n}(t)=i\left\langle n (t)\left|\nabla_{t}\right| n (t)\right\rangle \] 就称为 Berry联络; \[ B _{n}(t)=\nabla \times A _{n}(t) \] 为Berry曲率。

这里我们需要再次强调复归条件是很重要的，缺少此条件则几何相位就变得可通过规范变换任意调节而平庸地被吸收去掉了。因为我们可以对态矢作规范变 换 \(| n (t)\rangle \rightarrow e^{i \xi(t)}| n \rangle\), 于是就会对矢势相应造成改变 \(A _{n} \rightarrow A _{n}+i \nabla\), 然而 \(\nabla \times \nabla \xi=0\), 因此对复归系统的Berry相位不会造成影响。也就是说 \(\gamma_{n}(C)\) 是规范不变量，只依赖于闭路径 \(C\) 包含区域的几何性质，因此得名"几何相"。

二能级例子

一个磁场中的自旋系统哈密顿量 \[ H= B \cdot S \] 单自旋态矢可用相干态构造出 \[ n \cdot S | n \rangle=S| n \rangle \] 其中

  • \(| n \rangle=\left(z_{1}, z_{2}\right)^{T}\)
  • \(\left|z_{1}\right|^{2}+\left|z_{2}\right|^{2}=1\)
  • \(n =z^{\dagger} \sigma z\)
  • \(z=\left(e^{-i \phi} \cos \frac{\theta}{2}, \sin \frac{\theta}{2}\right)^{T}\)
  • \(\left\langle n _{b} \mid n _{a}\right\rangle=z_{b}^{\dagger} z_{a}\) 正交
  • \(\int \frac{d n }{2 \pi}| n \rangle\langle n |=1\) 完备

这时作用量写为 \[ S [ n (t)]=\int d t\left(i z^{\dagger} \dot{z}- B \cdot n S\right) \] 那么对于静磁场 \(B\), 自旋相干态不含时，因此总相位因子就只包含动力学相 \[ \left\langle n \left|e^{i \int_{0}^{T} d t B \cdot s }\right| n \right\rangle=e^{-i E_{0} T} \] 对于绝热变化并复归的磁场 \(B (t)\) 则有 \[ \begin{aligned} \left\langle n \left|e^{i S [ n (t)]}\right| n \right\rangle=& \lim _{N \rightarrow \infty}\langle n (T)|U(T, T-\Delta t)| n (T-\Delta t)\rangle \ldots \\ & \ldots\langle n (2 \Delta t)|U(2 \Delta t, \Delta t)| n (\Delta t)\rangle\langle n (\Delta t)|U(\Delta t, 0)| n (0)\rangle \\ =& \int D \left[\frac{ n (t)}{2 \pi}\right] e^{i S [ n (t)]}\\ =&=e^{-i E_{0} T} e^{i \int d t z^{\dagger} \dot{z}} \end{aligned} \] 可见这就多出了一个不依赖于时间变化的几何相位，并且在 \(B \rightarrow 0\) 时也不为零, 所以这个几何相位是纯粹的量子效应造成的。

将Hilbert空间映射到仅用 \(n (t)(\theta, \phi)\) 刻画的 \(S ^{2}\) Bloch球空间

\[ | n (\theta, \phi)\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{cc} \cos \frac{\theta}{2} e^{-i \phi} \\ \sin \frac{\theta}{2} \end{array}\right) \]

Berry曲率为 \[ \begin{aligned} &\quad \nabla \times\langle n \mid \nabla n \rangle \\ &=\left(\frac{1}{r} \partial_{\phi} e _{\phi}+\frac{1}{r \sin \phi} \partial_{\theta} e _{\theta}\right) \times z^{\dagger}\left(\frac{1}{r} \partial_{\phi} e _{\phi}+\frac{1}{r \sin \phi} \partial_{\theta} e _{\theta}\right) z \\ &=\frac{i}{2 r^{2}} e _{r} \end{aligned} \] 可清晰地看见几何相位正是Bloch球面上闭回路面积对应的立体角（和乐群元） \[ \begin{aligned} \gamma(C) &=-\frac{1}{2} \int \frac{1}{r^{2}} e _{r} \cdot d S \\ &=-\frac{1}{2} \int d \Omega\\ &=-\frac{1}{2} \Omega \end{aligned} \]

因此根据此几何图像，作用量可完全用 \(n\) 表示 \[ S [ n (t)]=-\oint_{\partial D} d t B \cdot n S+\int_{D} d t_{1} d t_{2} S n \cdot\left(\frac{d n }{d t_{1}} \times \frac{d n }{d t_{2}}\right) \] 除了动力学部分外 \(-\oint_{\partial D} d t B \cdot n S\)，

还有一个拓扑项，从这个拓扑项可得到自旋的运动方程： \[ \frac{\delta S}{\delta n (t)}=0 \Rightarrow \dot{ n }(t)= B \times n (t) \] 自旋系统的这种 \(O(3)\) 规范对称的 (Wess-Zumino ) 拓扑项

  1. 在一些磁性系统里面可以产生类似skyrmion的拓扑激发;
  2. 在拓扑超导系统中则可以产生Majorana零模激发。

量子Hall效应

一个晶格空间变化磁场作用在二维电子气体(2DEG) 上，电子会将绝热地随着局部磁场方向运动。那么在电子自身参考系看来就相当于有一个随时间绝热演化的磁场，因此也就会如同上面的模型那样造成Berry相位。下面我们来将此模型映射成垂直于晶格的有效磁场，那么这也就意味着Hall效应的出现，而Berry相位则带来拓扑Hall效应。

首先二维磁场晶格系统中电子运动的哈密顿量写为 \[ H=-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m^{*}} \nabla^{2}-g M (r) \cdot \sigma \] 紧束缚近似下哈密顿量为 \[ H=-t \sum_{\langle i j\rangle} \sum_{\alpha}|i \alpha\rangle \langle j \alpha |-g M \sum_{i} \sum_{\alpha, \beta} | i \alpha \rangle \sigma _{\alpha \beta} \cdot n _{i}\langle i \beta| \] 可定义基矢 \(\{|i, \pm\rangle\}, \quad+,-\) 为 \(i\) 格点上占据电子自旋与磁场平行、反平行状态: \[ |i,+\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{c} \cos \frac{\theta_{i}}{2} e^{-i \phi_{i}} \\ \sin \frac{\theta_{i}}{2} \end{array}\right), \quad|i,-\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{c} -\sin \frac{\theta_{i}}{2} \\ \cos \frac{\theta_{i}}{2} e^{i \phi_{i}} \end{array}\right) \] 定义投影算符 \[ P _{\pm}=\sum_{i}|i, \pm\rangle\langle i, \pm| \] 将整个系统投影到自旋上、下基矢张成的两个子空间中 \[ \begin{aligned} H &=H_{+} \oplus H_{-}\\ &=\left( P _{+}+ P _{-}\right) H\left( P _{+}+ P _{-}\right) \\ &= P _{+} H P _{+}+ P _{-} H P _{+}+ P _{+} H P _{-}+ P _{-} H P _{-} \end{aligned} \] 当Zeeman分裂能 \(\Delta=2 g M\) 足够大时，上自旋态便处于低能级，占据了几乎所有电子，而下自旋态则全空。电子自旋会绝热地跟随着晶格中局部磁场的方向而演化。 \[ H=\left(\begin{array}{cc} A & T \\ T^{\dagger} & A \end{array}\right) \] 其中 \[ A=\Delta\left(\begin{array}{cc} -\frac{1}{2} & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right) \]

\[ T=-t\left(\begin{array}{cc} \sin \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2}+\cos \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \cos \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{i\left(\phi_{1}-\phi_{2}\right)} & \sin \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{i \phi_{2}}-\cos \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{i \phi_{1}} \\ \sin \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{-i \phi_{1}}-\cos \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{-i \phi_{2}} & \sin \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \sin \frac{\theta_{2}}{2}+\cos \frac{\theta_{1}}{2} \cos \frac{\theta_{2}}{2} e^{-i\left(\phi_{1}-\phi_{2}\right)} \end{array}\right) \]

二维石墨烯（honeycomb）晶格

这时候Berry联络、曲率都是对Bloch的 \(U(1)\) 丛而言，Berry相位是与选取的 闭合路径有关的几何性相位角，如果该区域包含的是几何的整体那这个Berry 相位就反映整个流形 (或丛) 的拓扑性贡; 我们选取局部的闭合路径，那么就是反映该局部区域的几何性质。如前面的模型一样，几何上直观理解就再把这 个系统从二维Brillouin流形 \(T ^{2}\) 映射到Bloch球流形 \(S ^{2}\) : \[ \begin{aligned} H &=v_{F}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & k_{1}-i k_{2} \\ k_{1}+i k_{2} & 0 \end{array}\right) \\ &=v_{F} k\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & e^{-i \theta_{k}} \\ e^{i \theta_{k}} & 0 \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \] 解出Bloch态矢为 \[ \left|u^{+}\right\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{c} e^{-i \theta_{k}} \\ 1 \end{array}\right) \quad, \quad\left|u^{-}\right\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{c} -e^{-i \theta_{k}} \\ 1 \end{array}\right) \] 由此计算出Berry联络 \[ a_{k}^{+}=-i\left\langle u^{+}\left|\partial_{k}\right| u^{+}\right\rangle=0 \]

\[ a_{\theta_{k}}^{+}=-i\left\langle u^{+}\left|k^{-1} \partial_{\theta_{k}}\right| u^{+}\right\rangle=-\frac{1}{2 k} \]

这个Berry曲率就是含有非平庸的“磁单极”势 （演生拓扑缺陷） \[ b=\partial_{k} a_{\theta_{k}}-\partial_{\theta_{k}} a_{k}=\frac{1}{2 k^{2}} \] 积分路径包含这个拓扑非平庸的奇性区域则Berry相位为 \[ \begin{aligned} \gamma_{C}&=\iint_{S} b d^{2} k \\&=\oint_{C} a \cdot d k\\& =\int_{0}^{2 \pi} a_{\theta_{k}} k d \theta_{k}\\&=-\pi \end{aligned} \] 整体Berry相位就是等同于第一陈数（TKNN对IQHE提出的拓扑不变量就是基于此） \[ C_{1}=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \iint_{S} b d^{2} k=\gamma_{C} / 2 \pi=-\frac{1}{2} \]

Kane-Mele模型

映射后的 \(S U(2)\) 哈密顿量写为 \[ \begin{aligned} H(k) &=k_{x} \sigma^{x}+k_{y} \sigma^{y}+m \sigma^{z} \\ &=d_{i} \sigma^{i} \end{aligned} \] 那么极化矢量 \(d_{i}(k)\) 单位化后得到Bloch矢 \(\hat{d}_{i}(k)=\frac{d_{i}(k)}{\sqrt{d_{i} d^{i}}}\) 对角化此哈密顿量，解出Bloch态矢是 \[ \left|u^{-}\right\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{c} -\frac{k e^{-i \theta_{k}}}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(m+E_{k}\right)^{2}}} \\ \frac{E_{k}+m}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(E_{k}+m\right)^{2}}} \end{array}\right) \]

我们已经知道如果对系统的哈密顿量作规范变换，那么Berry相位不会变化，那Berry曲率（联络）方面会如何表现？

接下来我们考虑一个幺正变换 \(O(k)\) \[ H^{\prime}=O H O^{\dagger} \] 其中

  • \(H^{\prime}\) 和 \(H\) 的能谱是一样的

  • 能量为 \(E(k)\) 的态\(|u(k)\rangle\) 变成 \(\left|u^{\prime}(k)\right\rangle=O(k)|u(k)\rangle\)

现在Berry 联络变了，和原来相差 \[ a_{i}^{\prime}(k)-a_{i}(k)=-i\left\langle u^{\prime}(k)\left|\left(\partial_{k_{i}} O(k)\right) O^{\dagger}(k)\right| u^{\prime}(k)\right\rangle \]

具体地来对Kane-Mele模型

能量为 \[ E_{-}(k)=-\sqrt{|k|^{2}+m^{2}} \] 的态 \[ \left|u_{-}\right\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{c} -\frac{k e^{-i \theta_{k}}}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(m+E_{k}\right)^{2}}} \\ \frac{E_{k}+m}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(E_{k}+m\right)^{2}}} \end{array}\right) \] 作用幺正变换 \(O(k)=e^{i c k_{2} \sigma^{3}}\) 后 \[ \left|u_{-}^{\prime}\right\rangle=O(k)\left|u_{-}\right\rangle=\left(\begin{array}{c} -\frac{e^{i c k_{2}-i \theta_{k} k}}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(m+E_{k}\right)^{2}}} \\ \frac{e^{-i c k_{2}\left(m+E_{k}\right)}}{\sqrt{k^{2}+\left(m+E_{k}\right)^{2}}} \end{array}\right) \]

拓扑不变量的Green函数形式

详细

这个问题的起源是计算凝聚态系统中电流对于外电场的响应 \[ j_{\alpha}=\sum_{\beta} \sigma_{\alpha \beta} E_{\beta} \] 其中

  • \(\alpha, \beta=x, y\) 二维系统
  • \(\sigma_{\alpha \beta}\) 电导率

我们通常考虑外电场较弱的情况，此时我们可以采用含时微扰理论来处理，只需要考虑一阶项就可以了。这就是线性响应理论（Linear response theory）。为了简单起见，下面的讨论都基于零温情形。

微扰

由于矢勢 A 的强度较弱，因此我们李哈密顿量展开到关于 A 的一阶项 \[ \hat{H}(\mathbf{k}+e \mathbf{A} / \hbar) \simeq \hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\mathbf{A} \cdot \hat{\mathbf{j}}, \quad \hat{\mathbf{j}}=\frac{e}{\hbar} \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}} \]

\[ \hat{H}(\mathbf{k}+e \mathbf{A} / \hbar) \simeq \hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\mathbf{A} \cdot \hat{\mathbf{j}} \]

其中

  • \(\hat{\mathbf{j}}=\frac{e}{\hbar} \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\) 为电流密度算符
  • $ {n}(t)|| {n}(t) $ 在实验中真实测得的电流值

其中态矢量 \(\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle\) 满足 \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\mathrm{d} n_{0}}{\mathrm{d} \zeta}&=2 \sqrt{n_{0}\left(m_{1}-n_{0}\right)\left(m_{2}-n_{0}\right)-\left(m_{0}+\frac{1}{2} n_{0} \Delta s\right)^{2}} \\&=2 \sqrt{n_{0}\left(m_{1}-n_{0}\right)^{2}-\left(\frac{1}{2} n_{0} \Delta s\right)^{2}} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathrm{i} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle &=\left(\hat{H}_{0}+\hat{V}\right)\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \\&=\left[\hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\frac{e}{\hbar} \mathbf{A} \cdot \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\right]\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \end{aligned} \]

放到相互作用绘景中去讨论这个平均值，先对态矢量作变换 \(\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle\) 方程可以改写为 \[ \mathrm{i} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=e^{\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar} \hat{V} e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}(t)\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \] 这个方程的解可以通过反复迭代得到 \[ \left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=\left|\phi_{n}(-\infty)\right\rangle-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|\phi_{n}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right\rangle \] 近似，我们假定相互作用项是被绝热地从 \(t^{\prime}=-\infty\) 到 \(t^{\prime}=t\) 这个时间区间 内加上去的。此外, 还假定了 \(t^{\prime}=-\infty\) 的时候相互作用不存在, 因此 | \(\left.\phi_{n}(-\infty)\right\rangle\) 是 ${0} $ 的本征态。我们将这个态记为$ | u{n} |_{n}(-)$, 于是它满足方程 \(\hat{H}_{0}\left|u_{n}\right\rangle=\epsilon_{n}\left|u_{n}\right\rangle \cdot\) 在只考虑一阶微?的情形下 \[ \left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \simeq\left|u_{n}\right\rangle-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|u_{n}\right\rangle \] 原始的态矢量这个时候就可以被表示为 \[ \begin{aligned} \left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle&=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \\&=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}[1+S(t,-\infty)]\left|u_{n}\right\rangle \end{aligned} \] 其中 \(S(t,-\infty)=-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\)

电流算符的平均值

所以我们可以将电流算符的平均值表示为 \[ \begin{aligned} \langle\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)\rangle_{n} &=\left\langle\psi_{n}(t)|\hat{\mathbf{j}}| \psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \\&=\left\langle\phi_{n}(t)\left|e^{i \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar} \hat{\mathbf{j}} e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\right| \phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \\&=\left\langle\phi_{n}(t)|\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)| \phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \\&=\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[1+S^{\dagger}(t,-\infty)\right] \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)[1+S(t,-\infty)]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle \\& \simeq\left\langle u_{n}\left|S^{\dagger}(t,-\infty) \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)+\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t) S(t,-\infty)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle+\mathcal{O}\left(\hat{V}^{2}\right) \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right), \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)\right]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle \end{aligned} \] 在上面的计算中, 我们假定了$ u_n | (t)|u_{n}=0 .$ 这是显然的，因为不加外电场，处于基态 的系统当然不会自发产生电流。

系统的总电流可以通过对Fermi面以下的所有占据态求和得到 \(\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle_{n}\) \[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle_{n}=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right), \hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle \\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left[\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right) \hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t) \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\right] \\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\mathrm{\epsilon}_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \\& \times\left[\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\right] \\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \\& \times\left(e^{\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}-e^{-\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}\right) A_{\beta}\left(t^{\prime}\right) \end{aligned} \]

频率空间

将电流Fourier变换到频率空间进行讨论 \[ \begin{aligned} \left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(\omega)\right\rangle &=\int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} A_{\beta}(\omega)\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta}-\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{-\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta}\right] \\&=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} A_{\beta}(\omega)\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}+\hbar \omega+\mathrm{i} \eta}+\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}-\hbar \omega-\mathrm{i} \eta}\right] \end{aligned} \] 为了得到上面的式子，我们计算了如下积分 \[ \begin{aligned} & \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar} F\left(t^{\prime}\right) \\=& \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar} \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} e^{-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \\=& \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mp \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t / \hbar+\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t^{\prime} / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}+\eta t^{\prime}} \\=&\left.\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mp \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t / \hbar+\mathrm{i} \omega t} \frac{1}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime}+\eta} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t^{\prime} / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}+\eta^{\prime}}\right|_{-\infty} ^{t} \\=& \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i}\left(\omega-\omega^{\prime}\right) t} \frac{1}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime}+\eta} \\=& \frac{F(\omega)}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta} \end{aligned} \] 上面的计算中，为了使得积分收敘，我们引入了一个无限小的参数 \(\eta=0^{+}\)

作为 关联函数的电导

对于Bloch态, 我们可以将态矢量i记作 | \(\left.u_{n}\right\rangle=\left|u_{\mu}(\mathrm{k})\right\rangle\), 此时对态的求和则变为 \(\sum_{n}=\sum_{\mu, \mathbf{k}},\) 我们还需要注意到Maxwell方程组 \(\mathbf{E}=-\partial \mathbf{A} / \partial t\)，基对应的频率表示为 \(E_{\alpha}(\omega)=\mathrm{i} \omega A_{\alpha}\)

于是我们就得到了电导的表达式 \[ \sigma_{\alpha \beta}=\frac{\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)}{\mathrm{i} \omega} \] 其中 \(\prod_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)\) 流-流关联函数 \[ \Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}+\hbar \omega+\mathrm{i} \eta}+\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}-\hbar \omega-\mathrm{i} \eta}\right] \] 我们最后得到的电导一定是实数，因此我们只对关联函数的虚部感兴趣。

TKNN formula

我们接下来取直流极限 \(\omega \rightarrow 0\)。利用公式 \(\lim _{\eta \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{1}{\omega \pm \mathrm{i} \eta}=\mathrm{P} \frac{1}{\omega} \mp \mathrm{i} \pi \delta(\omega)\)，流-流关联函数 写成 \[ \begin{aligned} \Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0)=& \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}-\mathrm{i} \pi\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n} \delta\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\right. \\&\left.+\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}+\mathrm{i} \pi\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n} \delta\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\right] \\=& \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}+(n \leftrightarrow m)}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}\right] \end{aligned} \] 电导的计算公式变形为 \[ \begin{aligned} \sigma_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0) &=\lim _{\omega \rightarrow 0} \frac{\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)-\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(0)}{\mathrm{i} \omega}=\left.\frac{1}{\mathrm{i}} \frac{\partial \Pi_{\alpha \beta}}{\partial \omega}\right|_{\omega=0} \\&=\mathrm{i} \hbar \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}-\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}}{\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)^{2}} \end{aligned} \] 将流算符的表达式 \[ \left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}=\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle=\frac{e}{\hbar}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle \\\partial_{\alpha} \equiv \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{\alpha}}, \quad \alpha=x, y \] 代入电导公式 \[ \begin{aligned} \sigma_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0) &=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \frac{\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{m}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)^{2}} \\&=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\mu \nu} \sum_{\mathbf{k}} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}} \\&=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2} L^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\mu \nu} \int \frac{\mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y}}{(2 \pi)^{2}} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}} \end{aligned} \] 这就是标准的TKNN formula

  • 可以看出 \(\sigma_{\alpha \beta}=-\sigma_{\beta \alpha}\) 所以我们可以进一步将电导率写为 \(\sigma_{\alpha \beta}=\sigma_{\mathrm{H}} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\)。这里 \(\sigma_{\mathbf{H}}\) 表示Hall电导。
  • 注意到 \(\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\) 与 \(\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\) 互为复共轭的，因此

\[ \begin{aligned} &=\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle \\&=2 \operatorname{iIm}\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \end{aligned} \]

将这个等式代入TKNN公式

陈数

由此我们可以定义第一陈数（the first Chern number）的概念 \[ \begin{aligned} C_{\mu}^{\alpha \beta} &=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \sum_{\nu \neq \mu} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial^{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial^{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}} \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y}\left(\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right) \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \operatorname{tr}\left[\hat{P}_{\mu}\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}-\partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu}\right)\right] \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \end{aligned} \] 其中第三个等式的验证是 \[ \begin{aligned} &\operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right] \\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right]\left[\partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right] \\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|+\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right.\right.\right.\right.\right. \\&\left.+\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right] \\&=\epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right) \\&=\epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle \end{aligned} \] 如果我们讨论的是一般的二带模型（two-band model），那么第一陈数的计算可以直接从哈密顿量出发得到，而不需要先解出本征态。一般的二带模型的哈密顿量可以用Pauli矩阵展开为 \[ \hat{H}(\mathbf{k})=\sum_{i=x, y, z} d_{i}(\mathbf{k}) \hat{\sigma}_{i} \] 两个本征态可以表示为 \(\left|u_{\pm}\right\rangle\)。由此我们可以写出投影算符 \[ \hat{P}_{\pm}=\left|u_{\pm}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\pm}\right|=\frac{1}{2}\left(1 \pm \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right) \] 这是很容易验证的，我们只需要将哈密顿量作用到投影算符上 \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{P}_{\pm} \hat{H}=\hat{H} \hat{P}_{\pm} &=\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}+\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i} \hat{d}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{j} \\&=\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i} \pm \frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{j}\left(\mathrm{i} \sum_{k} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}+\delta_{i j} \hat{\sigma}_{0}\right) \end{aligned} \] 其中

  • 我们定义了单位矢量 \(\hat{d}_{i} \equiv d_{i} /|\mathbf{d}|\) ，并且满足 \(\sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{i}=1\)
  • 同时我们还注意到 \(\sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{j} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}=(\hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \hat{\mathbf{d}}) \cdot \hat{\vec{\sigma}}=0\)

所以不难看出存在这样的关系式 \(\hat{P}_{\pm} \hat{H}=\hat{H} \hat{P}_{\pm}=\pm|\mathbf{d}| \hat{P}_{\pm}\)。于是我们可以将上面的trace项直接求出来： \[ \begin{aligned} \operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} &=\operatorname{tr}\left(\hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}-\hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu}\right) \\ &=\frac{1}{8} \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left[\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right) \partial^{\alpha}\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{j}\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}\right)-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)\right] \\ &=\frac{1}{8} \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left[\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right)\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j}\right) \hat{\sigma}_{j}\left(\partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}\right) \hat{\sigma}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)\right] \\ &=\frac{1}{8} \mu \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left(\hat{\sigma}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{k}\right) \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta) \\ &=\frac{1}{8} \mu \sum_{i, j, k}\left(2 \mathrm{i} \epsilon_{i j k}\right) \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta) \\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{4} \sum_{i, j, k}\left(\epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-\epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{k}\right) \\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \sum_{i, j, k} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k} \\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathrm{d}}\right) \end{aligned} \] 代回到陈数的表达式，我们就得到了计算二带系统陈数的通用表达式 \[ \begin{aligned} C_{\mu}^{\alpha \beta}&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathbf{d}}\right) \\&=\frac{-\mu}{4 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathbf{d}}\right) \end{aligned} \]

简化

Berry phase： \[ \gamma_{m 2}(t)=\int_{0}^{t} d t^{\prime}\left\langle m\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|i \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} t^{\prime}}\right| m\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right\rangle \] 积分里面的部分（乘以i）被定义为Berry联络。引入矢量势 \(\vec{A}_{m}(\vec{R})=\left\langle m(\vec{R})\left|i \nabla_{\vec{R}}\right| m(\vec{R})\right\rangle\) 因此 Berry 相可写作 \[ \gamma_{m 2}(C)=\int_{C} \vec{A}_{m}(\vec{R}) \cdot d \vec{R}=\int_{S} \vec{B}_{m}(\vec{R}) \cdot d \vec{S} \] 这里引入的规范势其实为Berry截面上的联络，而规范场强为其截面的曲率，也就是我们所说的Berry曲率。

Berry联络的旋度就是Berry曲率 \[ \Omega_{n}=i \nabla_{k} \times\left\langle u_{n}\left|\nabla_{k}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle \] 展开 \[ \overrightarrow{B_{m}}(\vec{R})=i \sum_{n \neq m} \frac{\left\langle m(\vec{R})\left|\left(\nabla_{\vec{R}} \hat{H}(\vec{R})\right)\right| n(\vec{R})\right\rangle \times\left\langle n(\vec{R})\left|\left(\nabla_{\vec{R}} \hat{H}(\vec{R})\right)\right| m(\vec{R})\right\rangle}{\left(E_{m}(\vec{R})-E_{n}(\vec{R})\right)^{2}} \]
