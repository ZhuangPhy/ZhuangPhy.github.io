拓扑入门第一例

[TOC]

详细

这个问题的起源是计算凝聚态系统中电流对于外电场的响应
$$
j_{\alpha}=\sum_{\beta} \sigma_{\alpha \beta} E_{\beta}
$$
其中

  • $\alpha, \beta=x, y$ 二维系统
  • $\sigma_{\alpha \beta}$ 电导率

我们通常考虑外电场较弱的情况，此时我们可以采用含时微扰理论来处理，只需要考虑一阶项就可以了。这就是线性响应理论（Linear response theory）。为了简单起见，下面的讨论都基于零温情形。

微扰

由于矢勢 A 的强度较弱，因此我们李哈密顿量展开到关于 A 的一阶项
$$
\hat{H}(\mathbf{k}+e \mathbf{A} / \hbar) \simeq \hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\mathbf{A} \cdot \hat{\mathbf{j}}, \quad \hat{\mathbf{j}}=\frac{e}{\hbar} \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}}
$$

$$
\hat{H}(\mathbf{k}+e \mathbf{A} / \hbar) \simeq \hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\mathbf{A} \cdot \hat{\mathbf{j}}
$$

其中

  • $\hat{\mathbf{j}}=\frac{e}{\hbar} \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}}$ 为电流密度算符
  • $ \langle\psi_{n}(t)|\hat{\mathbf{j}}| \psi_{n}(t) \rangle$ 在实验中真实测得的电流值

其中态矢量 $\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle$ 满足
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\mathrm{d} n_{0}}{\mathrm{d} \zeta}&=2 \sqrt{n_{0}\left(m_{1}-n_{0}\right)\left(m_{2}-n_{0}\right)-\left(m_{0}+\frac{1}{2} n_{0} \Delta s\right)^{2}}
\\&=2 \sqrt{n_{0}\left(m_{1}-n_{0}\right)^{2}-\left(\frac{1}{2} n_{0} \Delta s\right)^{2}}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{i} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle &=\left(\hat{H}_{0}+\hat{V}\right)\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=\left[\hat{H}(\mathbf{k})+\frac{e}{\hbar} \mathbf{A} \cdot \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\right]\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\end{aligned}
$$

放到相互作用绘景中去讨论这个平均值，先对态矢量作变换 $\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle$ 方程可以改写为
$$
\mathrm{i} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=e^{\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar} \hat{V} e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}(t)\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
$$
这个方程的解可以通过反复迭代得到
$$
\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle=\left|\phi_{n}(-\infty)\right\rangle-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|\phi_{n}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right\rangle
$$
近似，我们假定相互作用项是被绝热地从 $t^{\prime}=-\infty$ 到 $t^{\prime}=t$ 这个时间区间 内加上去的。此外, 还假定了 $t^{\prime}=-\infty$ 的时候相互作用不存在, 因此 | $\left.\phi_{n}(-\infty)\right\rangle$ 是 $\hat{H}_{0} $ 的本征态。我们将这个态记为$ | u_{n} \rangle \equiv\left|\phi_{n}(-\infty)\right\rangle$, 于是它满足方程 $\hat{H}_{0}\left|u_{n}\right\rangle=\epsilon_{n}\left|u_{n}\right\rangle \cdot$ 在只考虑一阶微?的情形下
$$
\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle \simeq\left|u_{n}\right\rangle-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|u_{n}\right\rangle
$$
原始的态矢量这个时候就可以被表示为
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left|\psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle&=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\left|\phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}[1+S(t,-\infty)]\left|u_{n}\right\rangle
\end{aligned}
$$
其中 $S(t,-\infty)=-\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)$

电流算符的平均值

所以我们可以将电流算符的平均值表示为
$$
\begin{aligned}
\langle\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)\rangle_{n} &=\left\langle\psi_{n}(t)|\hat{\mathbf{j}}| \psi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=\left\langle\phi_{n}(t)\left|e^{i \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar} \hat{\mathbf{j}} e^{-\mathrm{i} \hat{H}_{0} / \hbar}\right| \phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=\left\langle\phi_{n}(t)|\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)| \phi_{n}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[1+S^{\dagger}(t,-\infty)\right] \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)[1+S(t,-\infty)]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle
\\& \simeq\left\langle u_{n}\left|S^{\dagger}(t,-\infty) \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)+\hat{\mathbf{j}}(t) S(t,-\infty)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle+\mathcal{O}\left(\hat{V}^{2}\right)
\\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right), \hat{\mathbf{j}}(t)\right]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle
\end{aligned}
$$
在上面的计算中, 我们假定了$ \langle u_n | \hat{\mathrm{j}}(t)\left|u_{n}\right\rangle=0 .$ 这是显然的，因为不加外电场，处于基态 的系统当然不会自发产生电流。

系统的总电流可以通过对Fermi面以下的所有占据态求和得到 $\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle_{n}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle_{n}=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\left[\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right), \hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right]\right| u_{n}\right\rangle
\\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}\left[\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right) \hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t) \hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\right]
\\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\mathrm{\epsilon}_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}
\\& \times\left[\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\hat{V}_{\mathrm{I}}\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\right]
\\=& \frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime}
\\& \times\left(e^{\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}-e^{-\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}\right) A_{\beta}\left(t^{\prime}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

频率空间

将电流Fourier变换到频率空间进行讨论
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(\omega)\right\rangle &=\int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t}\left\langle\hat{j}_{\alpha}(t)\right\rangle
\\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} A_{\beta}(\omega)\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta}-\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{-\mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta}\right]
\\&=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \sum_{\beta} A_{\beta}(\omega)\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}+\hbar \omega+\mathrm{i} \eta}+\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}-\hbar \omega-\mathrm{i} \eta}\right]
\end{aligned}
$$
为了得到上面的式子，我们计算了如下积分
$$
\begin{aligned}
& \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar} F\left(t^{\prime}\right)
\\=& \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\left(t^{\prime}-t\right) / \hbar} \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} e^{-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right)
\\=& \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mp \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t / \hbar+\mathrm{i} \omega t} \int_{-\infty}^{t} \mathrm{d} t^{\prime} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t^{\prime} / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}+\eta t^{\prime}}
\\=&\left.\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mp \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t / \hbar+\mathrm{i} \omega t} \frac{1}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime}+\eta} e^{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) t^{\prime} / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime} t^{\prime}+\eta^{\prime}}\right|_{-\infty} ^{t}
\\=& \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} \omega^{\prime} F\left(\omega^{\prime}\right) \int \mathrm{d} t e^{\mathrm{i}\left(\omega-\omega^{\prime}\right) t} \frac{1}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega^{\prime}+\eta}
\\=& \frac{F(\omega)}{\pm \mathrm{i}\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right) / \hbar-\mathrm{i} \omega+\eta}
\end{aligned}
$$
上面的计算中，为了使得积分收敘，我们引入了一个无限小的参数 $\eta=0^{+}$

作为 关联函数的电导

对于Bloch态, 我们可以将态矢量i记作 | $\left.u_{n}\right\rangle=\left|u_{\mu}(\mathrm{k})\right\rangle$, 此时对态的求和则变为 $\sum_{n}=\sum_{\mu, \mathbf{k}},$ 我们还需要注意到Maxwell方程组 $\mathbf{E}=-\partial \mathbf{A} / \partial t$，基对应的频率表示为 $E_{\alpha}(\omega)=\mathrm{i} \omega A_{\alpha}$

于是我们就得到了电导的表达式
$$
\sigma_{\alpha \beta}=\frac{\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)}{\mathrm{i} \omega}
$$
其中 $\prod_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)$ 流-流关联函数
$$
\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)=\sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}+\hbar \omega+\mathrm{i} \eta}+\frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}-\hbar \omega-\mathrm{i} \eta}\right]
$$
我们最后得到的电导一定是实数，因此我们只对关联函数的虚部感兴趣。

TKNN formula

我们接下来取直流极限 $\omega \rightarrow 0$。利用公式 $\lim _{\eta \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{1}{\omega \pm \mathrm{i} \eta}=\mathrm{P} \frac{1}{\omega} \mp \mathrm{i} \pi \delta(\omega)$，流-流关联函数 写成
$$
\begin{aligned}
\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0)=& \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}-\mathrm{i} \pi\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n} \delta\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\right.
\\&\left.+\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}+\mathrm{i} \pi\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n} \delta\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)\right]
\\=& \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m}\left[\mathrm{P} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}+(n \leftrightarrow m)}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}\right]
\end{aligned}
$$
电导的计算公式变形为
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sigma_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0) &=\lim _{\omega \rightarrow 0} \frac{\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(\omega)-\Pi_{\alpha \beta}(0)}{\mathrm{i} \omega}=\left.\frac{1}{\mathrm{i}} \frac{\partial \Pi_{\alpha \beta}}{\partial \omega}\right|_{\omega=0}
\\&=\mathrm{i} \hbar \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \frac{\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{m n}-\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{m n}\left[\hat{j}_{\beta}\right]_{n m}}{\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)^{2}}
\end{aligned}
$$
将流算符的表达式
$$
\left[\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right]_{n m}=\left\langle u_{n}\left|\hat{j}_{\alpha}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle=\frac{e}{\hbar}\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle
\\\partial_{\alpha} \equiv \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{\alpha}}, \quad \alpha=x, y
$$
代入电导公式
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sigma_{\alpha \beta}(\omega \rightarrow 0) &=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\epsilon_{n}<\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}} \sum_{m} \frac{\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{m}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{m}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{n}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{m}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}\right)^{2}}
\\&=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\mu \nu} \sum_{\mathbf{k}} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}}
\\&=\mathrm{i} \frac{e^{2} L^{2}}{\hbar} \sum_{\mu \nu} \int \frac{\mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y}}{(2 \pi)^{2}} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}}
\end{aligned}
$$
这就是标准的TKNN formula

  • 可以看出 $\sigma_{\alpha \beta}=-\sigma_{\beta \alpha}$ 所以我们可以进一步将电导率写为 $\sigma_{\alpha \beta}=\sigma_{\mathrm{H}} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}$。这里 $\sigma_{\mathbf{H}}$ 表示Hall电导。
  • 注意到 $\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle$ 与 $\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle$ 互为复共轭的，因此

$$
\begin{aligned}
&=\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle
\\&=2 \operatorname{iIm}\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial_{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial_{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle
\end{aligned}
$$

将这个等式代入TKNN公式

陈数

由此我们可以定义第一陈数（the first Chern number）的概念
$$
\begin{aligned}
C_{\mu}^{\alpha \beta} &=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \sum_{\nu \neq \mu} \frac{\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial^{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\beta} \hat{H}\right| u_{\nu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\nu}\left|\partial^{\alpha} \hat{H}\right| u_{\mu}\right\rangle}{\left(\epsilon_{\mu}-\epsilon_{\nu}\right)^{2}}
\\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y}\left(\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle-\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right) \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \operatorname{tr}\left[\hat{P}_{\mu}\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}-\partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu}\right)\right] \\&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}
\end{aligned}
$$
其中第三个等式的验证是
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}
\\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right]
\\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right]\left[\partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right]
\\&=\operatorname{tr}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left[\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|\partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}\left|+\partial^{\alpha}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right.\right.\right.\right.\right.
\\&\left.+\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)\left\langle u_{\mu}|+| u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\alpha}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\left|u_{\mu}\right\rangle \partial^{\beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu}\right|\right)\right]
\\&=\epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left(\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle u_{\mu} | \partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle+\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle\left\langle\partial^{\beta} u_{\mu} | u_{\mu}\right\rangle\right)
\\&=\epsilon_{\alpha \beta}\left\langle\partial^{\alpha} u_{\mu} | \partial^{\beta} u_{\mu}\right\rangle
\end{aligned}
$$
如果我们讨论的是一般的二带模型（two-band model），那么第一陈数的计算可以直接从哈密顿量出发得到，而不需要先解出本征态。一般的二带模型的哈密顿量可以用Pauli矩阵展开为
$$
\hat{H}(\mathbf{k})=\sum_{i=x, y, z} d_{i}(\mathbf{k}) \hat{\sigma}_{i}
$$
两个本征态可以表示为 $\left|u_{\pm}\right\rangle$。由此我们可以写出投影算符
$$
\hat{P}_{\pm}=\left|u_{\pm}\right\rangle\left\langle u_{\pm}\right|=\frac{1}{2}\left(1 \pm \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right)
$$
这是很容易验证的，我们只需要将哈密顿量作用到投影算符上
$$
\begin{aligned}
\hat{P}_{\pm} \hat{H}=\hat{H} \hat{P}_{\pm} &=\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}+\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i} \hat{d}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{j}
\\&=\frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i} \pm \frac{1}{2}|\mathbf{d}| \sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{j}\left(\mathrm{i} \sum_{k} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}+\delta_{i j} \hat{\sigma}_{0}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$
其中

  • 我们定义了单位矢量 $\hat{d}_{i} \equiv d_{i} /|\mathbf{d}|$ ，并且满足 $\sum_{i} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{i}=1$
  • 同时我们还注意到 $\sum_{i, j} \hat{d}_{i} \hat{d}_{j} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}=(\hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \hat{\mathbf{d}}) \cdot \hat{\vec{\sigma}}=0$

所以不难看出存在这样的关系式 $\hat{P}_{\pm} \hat{H}=\hat{H} \hat{P}_{\pm}=\pm|\mathbf{d}| \hat{P}_{\pm}$。于是我们可以将上面的trace项直接求出来：
$$
\begin{aligned} \operatorname{tr} \epsilon_{\alpha \beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} &=\operatorname{tr}\left(\hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu}-\hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\beta} \hat{P}_{\mu} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{P}_{\mu}\right)
\\ &=\frac{1}{8} \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left[\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right) \partial^{\alpha}\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{j}\right) \partial^{\beta}\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{k} \hat{\sigma}_{k}\right)-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)\right]
\\ &=\frac{1}{8} \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left[\left(1+\mu \hat{d}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{i}\right)\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j}\right) \hat{\sigma}_{j}\left(\partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}\right) \hat{\sigma}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)\right]
\\ &=\frac{1}{8} \mu \sum_{i, j, k} \operatorname{tr}\left(\hat{\sigma}_{i} \hat{\sigma}_{j} \hat{\sigma}_{k}\right) \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)
\\ &=\frac{1}{8} \mu \sum_{i, j, k}\left(2 \mathrm{i} \epsilon_{i j k}\right) \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-(\alpha \leftrightarrow \beta)
\\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{4} \sum_{i, j, k}\left(\epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}-\epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{k}\right)
\\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \sum_{i, j, k} \epsilon_{i j k} \hat{d}_{i} \partial^{\alpha} \hat{d}_{j} \partial^{\beta} \hat{d}_{k}
\\ &=\frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathrm{d}}\right) \end{aligned}
$$
代回到陈数的表达式，我们就得到了计算二带系统陈数的通用表达式
$$
\begin{aligned}
C_{\mu}^{\alpha \beta}&=\frac{\mathrm{i}}{2 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \frac{\mathrm{i} \mu}{2} \hat{\mathrm{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathbf{d}}\right)
\\&=\frac{-\mu}{4 \pi} \int \mathrm{d} k_{x} \mathrm{d} k_{y} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \cdot\left(\partial^{\alpha} \hat{\mathbf{d}} \times \partial^{\beta} \hat{\mathbf{d}}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

概述

Berry phase：
$$
\gamma_{m 2}(t)=\int_{0}^{t} d t^{\prime}\left\langle m\left(t^{\prime}\right)\left|i \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} t^{\prime}}\right| m\left(t^{\prime}\right)\right\rangle
$$
积分里面的部分（乘以i）被定义为Berry联络。引入矢量势 $\vec{A}_{m}(\vec{R})=\left\langle m(\vec{R})\left|i \nabla_{\vec{R}}\right| m(\vec{R})\right\rangle$ 因此 Berry 相可写作
$$
\gamma_{m 2}(C)=\int_{C} \vec{A}_{m}(\vec{R}) \cdot d \vec{R}=\int_{S} \vec{B}_{m}(\vec{R}) \cdot d \vec{S}
$$
这里引入的规范势其实为Berry截面上的联络，而规范场强为其截面的曲率，也就是我们所说的Berry曲率。

Berry联络的旋度就是Berry曲率
$$
\Omega_{n}=i \nabla_{k} \times\left\langle u_{n}\left|\nabla_{k}\right| u_{n}\right\rangle
$$
展开
$$
\overrightarrow{B_{m}}(\vec{R})=i \sum_{n \neq m} \frac{\left\langle m(\vec{R})\left|\left(\nabla_{\vec{R}} \hat{H}(\vec{R})\right)\right| n(\vec{R})\right\rangle \times\left\langle n(\vec{R})\left|\left(\nabla_{\vec{R}} \hat{H}(\vec{R})\right)\right| m(\vec{R})\right\rangle}{\left(E_{m}(\vec{R})-E_{n}(\vec{R})\right)^{2}}
$$
