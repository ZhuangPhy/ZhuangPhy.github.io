2.1 证明

证明 \([\hat{a}, \hat{a}]=0,\left[\hat{a}^{\dagger}, \hat{a}^{\dagger}\right]=0,\left[\hat{a}, \hat{a}^{\dagger}\right]=1\) 和 \(\hat{H}=\hbar \omega\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\frac{1}{2}\right)\) 。

\[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{a}, \hat{a}^{\dagger}\right] &=\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}\left(-\frac{i}{m \omega}[\hat{x}, \hat{p}]+\frac{i}{m \omega}[\hat{p}, \hat{x}]\right) \\ &=\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}\left(\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}+\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}\right) \\ &=\hbar \omega\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\frac{1}{2}\right) \end{aligned} \]

2.2 能量本征值

题目: 对哈密顿量 \(\hat{H}=\frac{\hat{p}^{2}}{2 m}+\frac{1}{2} m \omega^{2} \hat{x}^{2}+\lambda \hat{x}^{4}\), 其中 \(\lambda\) 很小, 把这个哈密顿量用产生湮灭算符表示，并且使用微扰论证明能量本征值为： \[ E_{n}=\left(n+\frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega+\frac{3 \lambda}{4}\left(\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}\right)^{2}\left(2 n^{2}+2 n+1\right) \]

  • 前两项即为简谐振子
  • 对于微扰项 \(\lambda \hat{x}^{4}\), 把其中的位置算符用产生江灭算符表示出来有：

\[ \begin{aligned} \lambda \hat{x}^{4}=& \lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)\left(\hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{4} \\ =& \lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)\left(\hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\right.\\ & \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\\ &\left.\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger}\right) \end{aligned} \]

展开之后一共有 16 项，根据 1 阶微扰对能量的修正公式 \[ E_{n}^{(1)}=\left\langle n\left|H^{\prime}\right| n\right\rangle=\left\langle n\left|\lambda \hat{x}^{4}\right| n\right\rangle \] 以及 \[ \begin{array}{c} \hat{a}|n\rangle=\sqrt{n}|n-1\rangle \\ \hat{a}^{\dagger}|n\rangle=\sqrt{n+1}|n+1\rangle \end{array} \] 知道，只有被微扰哈密顿量作用之后与初始时一样的态才会被保留，也 即只有 6 项在考虑 1 阶微扰时候不为零，所以上面式子可以化简为: \[ \begin{aligned} \lambda \hat{x}^{4} &=\lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)\left(\hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{4} \\ &=\lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)\left(\hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a} \hat{a} \hat{a}^{\dagger}\right) \\ &=\lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)(n+1)(n+2)+n(n-1)+(n+1)^{2}+n^{2}+(n+1) n+n(n+1) \\ &=\lambda\left(\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}\right)\left(6 n^{2}+6 n+3\right) \end{aligned} \] 小结-能量本征值为： \[ E_{n}=\left(n+\frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega+\frac{3 \lambda}{4}\left(\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}\right)^{2}\left(2 n^{2}+2 n+1\right) \]

2.3 坐标算符的傅里叶

证明 \[ \hat{x}_{j}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\left(\frac{\hbar}{m}\right) \sum_{k} \frac{1}{\left(2 \omega_{k}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}}\left[\hat{a}_{k} e^{i k_{j} a}+\hat{a}_{k}^{\dagger} e^{-i k_{j} a}\right] \]

证明

用升降算符表示坐标 \(\hat{x}_{k}=\sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega_{k}}}\left(\hat{a}_{k}+\hat{a}_{-k}^{\dagger}\right)\)，代入傅里叶级数的系数中 \[ \begin{aligned} x_{j} &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}} \sum_{k} \hat{x}_{k} e^{i k j a} \\ &=\sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m N}} \sum_{k} \frac{1}{\omega_{k}}\left(\hat{a}_{-k}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}_{k}\right) e^{i k j a} \\ &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\left(\frac{\hbar}{m}\right) \sum_{k} \frac{1}{\left(2 \omega_{k}\right)^{1 / 2}}\left[\hat{a}_{k} e^{i k_{j} a}+\hat{a}_{k}^{\dagger} e^{-i k j a}\right] \end{aligned} \]

2.4 产生湮灭算符用来解波函

使用 \(\hat{a}|0\rangle=0\), 公式\(\hat{a}=\sqrt{\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}+\frac{ i }{m \omega} \hat{p}\right)\), 公式\(\hat{a}^{\dagger}=\sqrt{\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}-\frac{ i }{m \omega} \hat{p}\right)\) 以及 $x|p| =-i x $ 证明: \[ 0=\left(x+\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}\right) \frac{ d }{ d x}\langle x \mid \psi\rangle \] 并且由此得到： \[ \langle x \mid \psi\rangle=\left(\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}\right)^{1 / 4} e^{-m \omega x^{2} / 2 \hbar} \]

对于基态 \[ \hat{a}|0\rangle= 0 \] 升降算符 转换成 坐标算符和动量算符 \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{a} u_{0}(x) &=\left[\sqrt{\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}+\frac{i}{m \omega} \hat{p}\right)\right] u_{0}(x) \\ 0&=\left[\sqrt{\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}+\frac{\hbar}{m \omega} \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x}\right)\right] u_{0}(x) \\ \frac{\hbar}{m \omega} \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x} u_{0}(x)&=-x u_{0}(x) \\ \frac{\mathrm{d}u_{0}(x)}{u_{0}(x) } &=-\frac{m \omega}{\hbar} x \mathrm{d} x \\ {u_{0}(x) } &=\left(\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}\right)^{1 / 4} e^{-m \omega x^{2} / 2 \hbar} \end{aligned} \]

3.6 (1) 玻色算符证明

用对易关系\(\left[\hat{a}_{p}, \hat{a}_{q}^{\dagger}\right]=\delta_{p q}\)证明 \[ \frac{1}{\mathscr{V}} \sum_{p q} \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}(\boldsymbol{p} \cdot \boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{q} \cdot \boldsymbol{y})}\left[\hat{a}_{p}, \hat{a}_{q}^{\dagger}\right]=\delta^{(3)}(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{y}) \] 证明 \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{\mathscr{V}} \sum_{p q} e^{i(p \cdot x-q \cdot y)}\left[\hat{a}_{p}, \hat{a}_{q}^{\dagger}\right] &=\frac{1}{\mathscr{V}} \sum_{p q} e^{i(p \cdot x-q \cdot y)} \delta_{p q} \\ &=\frac{1}{\mathscr{V}} \sum_{p} e^{ip \cdot(x-y)} \\ &=\delta^{(3)}(x-y) \end{aligned} \]

关键点：狄拉克函数\(\delta_{p q}\)求和的性质

3.6 (2)证明一些升级算符的性质

    1. \(\left[a,\left(a^{\dagger}\right)^{n}\right]=n\left(a^{\dagger}\right)^{n-1}\)
    1. \(\left\langle 0\left|\hat{a}^{n}\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{m}\right| 0\right\rangle= n ! \delta_{n m}\)
    1. \(\left\langle m\left|\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right| n\right\rangle=\sqrt{n+1} \delta_{m, n+1}\)
    1. \(\langle m|\hat{a}| n\rangle=\sqrt{n} \delta_{m, n-1}\)

（a）

归纳法

  • 对于\(n=1\)，假设成立

\[ \left[a,\left(a^{\dagger}\right)^{1}\right]=1\left(a^{\dagger}\right)^{1-1}=1 \]

  • 对于\(n=k+1\)，有

\[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{a},\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k+1}\right] &=\hat{a}\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k+1}-\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k+1} \hat{a} \\ &=\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger} \hat{a}+1\right)\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k}-\hat{a}^{\dagger}\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k} \hat{a} \\ &=\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k}+\hat{a}^{\dot{+}}\left[\hat{a},\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k}\right] \\ &=\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k}+\hat{a}^{\dagger} k\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k-1} \\ &=(k+1)\left(\hat{a}^{\dagger}\right)^{k} \end{aligned} \]

  1. \[ \left\langle 0\left|\hat{a}^{n}\left(\hat{a}^{\dot{\top}}\right)^{m}\right| 0\right\rangle=\sqrt{n ! m !}\langle n | m\rangle= n ! \delta_{n m} \]

  2. \[ \left\langle m\left|\hat{a}^{\frac{\lambda}{4}}\right| n\right\rangle=\sqrt{n+1}\langle m | n+1\rangle=\sqrt{n+1} \delta_{m, n+1} \]

  3. \[ \langle m|\hat{a}| n\rangle=\sqrt{n}\langle m | n-1\rangle=\sqrt{n} \delta_{m, n-1} \]

3.6 (3)基地变换

三维谐振子的哈密顿量如下 \[ \hat{H}=\frac{1}{2 m}\left(\hat{p}_{1}^{2}+\hat{p}_{2}^{2}+\hat{p}_{3}^{2}\right)+\frac{1}{2} m \omega^{2}\left(\hat{x}_{1}^{2}+\hat{x}_{2}^{2}+\hat{x}_{3}^{2}\right) \] 定义产生算符 \(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}, \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\) 和 \(\hat{a}_{3}^{\dagger}\) 使得 \(\left[\hat{a}_{i}, \hat{a}_{j}^{\dagger}\right]=\delta_{i, j}\), 并证明 \[ \hat{H}=\hbar \omega \sum_{i=1}^{3}\left(\hat{a}_{i}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{i}+\frac{1}{2}\right) \] 角动量定义为 \[ \hat{L}^{i}=- i \hbar \epsilon^{i j k} \hat{a}_{j}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{k} \] 比如 \(\hat{L}^{3}=- i \hbar\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}-\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}\right)_{\circ}\) 定义新的产生算符为 \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{b}_{1}^{\dagger} &=-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right) \\ \hat{b}_{0}^{\dagger} &=\hat{a}_{3}^{\dagger} \\ \hat{b}_{-1}^{\dagger} &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right) \end{aligned} \] 证明 \(\left[\hat{b}_{i}, \hat{b}_{j}^{\dagger}\right]=\delta_{i, j}\), 哈密顿量可以改写为 \(\hat{H}=\hbar \omega \sum_{-1}^{1}\left(\hat{b}_{m}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{m}+\frac{1}{2}\right)\), 角动 量可以改写为 \(\hat{L}^{3}=\hbar \sum_{m=-1}^{1} m \hat{b}_{m}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{m \circ}\)

哈密顿量可以视为三个解耦的一维谐振子

\[ \hat{H}=\hbar \omega \sum_{i=1}^{3}\left(\hat{a}_{i}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{i}+\frac{1}{2}\right) \] 新算符的对易性质 \[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{b}_{1}, \hat{b}_{1}^{\dagger}\right] &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+i \hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}-\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\left[\hat{a}_{1}, \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}\right]+\left[\hat{a}_{2}, \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right]\right) \\ &=1 \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{b}_{-1}, \hat{b}_{-1}^{\dagger}\right] &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}-i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+\hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}-\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=1 \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{b}_{1}, \hat{b}_{-1}^{\dagger}\right] &=-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}+\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=0 \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \left[\hat{b}_{-1}, \hat{b}_{1}^{\dagger}\right] &=-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{1} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{2} \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}-\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}+\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=0 \end{aligned} \]

由此证明了 \(\left[\hat{b}_{i}, \hat{b}_{j}^{\dagger}\right]=\delta_{i j}\) \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{b}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{1}+\hat{b}_{-1}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{-1} &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &=\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}+\hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2} \\ \hat{b}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{1}-\hat{b}_{-1}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{-1} &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}+ i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{2}\right)-\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger}- i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger}\right)\left(\hat{a}_{1}+ i \hat{a}_{2}\right) \\ &= i \hat{a}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{1}- i \hat{a}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{a}_{2} \end{aligned} \] 因此 \[ \hat{H}=\hbar \omega \sum_{m=-1}^{1}\left(\frac{1}{2}+\hat{b}_{m}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{m}\right), \quad \hat{L}^{3}=\hbar \sum_{m=-1}^{1} m \hat{b}_{m}^{\dagger} \hat{b}_{m} \]

3.6 (4)

对于费米子，因为有 \(\hat{c}_{1}^{\dagger} \hat{c}_{2}^{\dagger}|0\rangle=-\hat{c}_{2}^{\dagger} \hat{c}_{1}^{\dagger}|0\rangle\), 因此定义 \(|11\rangle\) 的时候必须指明算符的作用顺序。如果我们让第一个费米子处于 \(\left|\psi_{1}\right\rangle\), 第二个 处于 \(\left|\psi_{2}\right\rangle\), 那么 \(|11\rangle\) 对应的波函数就是 \[ \Psi\left(r_{1}, r_{2}\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left[\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)-\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right] \] 对于一般情况，证明 \(N\) 个费米子的波函数可以被写成斯莱特（Slater) 行列式： \[ \frac{1}{\sqrt{N !}}\left|\begin{array}{cccc} \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) & \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) & \cdots & \psi_{N}\left(r_{1}\right) \\ \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right) & \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right) & \cdots & \psi_{N}\left(r_{2}\right) \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ \psi_{1}\left(r_{N}\right) & \psi_{2}\left(r_{N}\right) & \cdots & \psi_{N}\left(r_{N}\right) \end{array}\right| \]
