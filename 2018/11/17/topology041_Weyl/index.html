外尔半金属最小模型

外尔点：能带在动量空间某处相交，而交点的能量又恰好在费米能级附近

外尔半金属最小模型的哈密顿量 ： \[ H=A\left(k_{x} \sigma_{x}+k_{y} \sigma_{y}\right)+M_{k} \sigma_{z} \] 其中

  • \(M_{k}=M_{0}-M_{1}\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{y}^{2}+k_{z}^{2}\right)\)

解得哈密顿量的本征值为： \[ E_{\pm}=\pm \sqrt{M_{k}^{2}+A^{2}\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{y}^{2}\right)} \] 根据外尔点的定义：两个能带相交的点 \[ \begin{aligned} - \sqrt{M_{k}^{2}+A^{2}\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{y}^{2}\right)}&= \sqrt{M_{k}^{2}+A^{2}\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{y}^{2}\right)} \end{aligned} \] 所以

  • $A{2}(k_{x}{2}+k_{y}^{2})=0 k_{x}=k_{y}=0 $
  • $M_{k}=0 M_{0}-M_{1} k_{z}^{2}=0 $

把符合要求的动量定义为 \(k_z=k_{c} \equiv \sqrt{M_{0} / M_{1}}\)，于是我们得到两个外尔点的坐标为 \[ \left(k_{x} +k_{y} +k_{z} \right)=\left(0,0, \pm k_{c}\right) \]

数值

根据数值结果可以看出，在费米能级附近能带有两个交点。(代码见附录)

表面态：费米线

y=0表面态的哈密顿量： \[ E_{\operatorname{arc}}\left(k_{x}, k_{z}\right)=\operatorname{sgn}(M) A k_{x} \] 表面态还有以下限制： \[ k_{x}^{2}+k_{z}^{2}<k_{c}^{2} \] 在这里给定一个费米能, 表面态 (费米弧) 是一条线段。当费米能 \(E=0\) 时 \(,\) \(k_{x}=0,\) 该线段为 \(\left|k_{z}\right|<\left|k_{c}\right|,\) 连接两个外尔点。

数值计算结果

费米弧

外尔半金属最小模型的另一个模型 \[ H=A\left(k_{x} \sigma_{x}+k_{y} \sigma_{y}\right)+M\left(k_{w}^{2}-k^{2}\right) \sigma_{z} \] 该形式与上面那种形式 \(H=A\left(k_{x} \sigma_{x}+k_{y} \sigma_{y}\right)+M_{k} \sigma_{z}\) 是等价的。对应关系为：

  • \(M_{0} \rightarrow M k_{w}^{2}\)
  • \(M_{1} \rightarrow M\)

这里外尔点的坐标为： \(\left(0,0, \pm k_{w}\right)\)

加入平庸项后哈密顿量写为： \[ H=D_{1} k_{y}^{2}+D_{2}\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{z}^{2}\right)+A\left(k_{x} \sigma_{x}+k_{y} \sigma_{y}\right)+M\left(k_{w}^{2}-k^{2}\right) \sigma_{z} \] 通过推导, 可得到y=0表面态的哈密顿量： \[ H_{ arc }=D_{1} k_{w}^{2}+v k_{x}+\left(D_{2}-D_{1}\right)\left(k_{x}^{2}+k_{z}^{2}\right) \] 其中

  • \(v \equiv A \sqrt{M^{2}-D_{1}^{2}} / M\)

同时在推导过程中，表面态还有以下限制： \[ k_{x}^{2}+k_{z}^{2}+2 a k_{x}<k_{w}^{2} \] 其中

  • \(a \equiv A D_{1} / 2 M \sqrt{M^{2}-D_{1}^{2}}\)

从表面态哈密顿量可以看出，这时候给定一个费米能，表面态不再是一个线段，而是在限制条件下的一段曲线，称为费米弧。

陈数的计算

外尔半金属的拓扑数计算

现在我们计算 \(H( k )= k \cdot \sigma\) 对应的陈数，

1哈密顿量

\[ \begin{aligned} H( k )&=\pm k \cdot \sigma \\&=\pm\left(k_{x} \sigma_{x}+k_{y} \sigma_{y}+k_{z} \sigma_{s}\right) \\&=k_{x}\left(\begin{array}{ll} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array}\right)+k_{y}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{array}\right)+k_{z}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right) \\&=\left(\begin{array}{cc} k_{s} & k_{x}-i k_{y} \\ k_{x}+i k_{y} & -k_{z} \end{array}\right) \end{aligned} \]

2将直角坐标转到球面坐标

\(k_{x}=k \sin \theta \cos \phi,\)$ k_{y}=k ,\[ \quad k_{z}=k \cos \theta,$ 其中$ k=| k |, \quad$ 则 \] \[\begin{aligned} H( k )&=\left(\begin{array}{cc} k \cos \theta & k \sin \theta \cos \phi-i k \sin \theta \sin \phi \\ k \sin \theta \cos \phi+i k \sin \theta \sin \phi & -k \cos \theta \end{array}\right) \\&=\left(\begin{array}{cc} k \cos \theta & k \sin \theta e^{-i \phi} \\ k \sin \theta e^{i \phi} & -k \cos \theta \end{array}\right) \end{aligned}\]

$$

3 求本征态

哈密顿量对应的久期方程为 \[ \left|\begin{array}{cc} k \cos \theta-E & k \sin \theta e^{-i \phi} \\ k \sin \theta e^{i \phi} & -k \cos \theta-E \end{array}\right|=0 \] 解久期方程得到系统的本征值为 \[ E=\pm k \] 我们解本征值为 \(-k\) 对应的本征矢 \(\psi=\left(\begin{array}{l}a \\ b\end{array}\right),\) 哈密顿量为 \[ \left(\begin{array}{cc} k \cos \theta & k \sin \theta e^{-i \phi} \\ k \sin \theta e^{i \phi} & -k \cos \theta \end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{l} a \\ b \end{array}\right)=-k\left(\begin{array}{l} a \\ b \end{array}\right) \] 可以解得本征矢为 \[ \psi=\left(\begin{array}{c} -\sin \frac{\theta}{2} e^{-i \phi} \\ \cos \frac{\theta}{2} \end{array}\right) \]

4 贝里联络

梯度算符在球坐标中定义为 \[ \nabla_{ k }=\frac{\partial}{\partial k} e _{k}+\frac{1}{k} \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} e _{\theta}+\frac{1}{k \sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} e _{\phi} \] 可以计算系统的贝里联络为 \[ \begin{aligned} A( k )&=\left\langle\psi\left|i \nabla_{ k }\right| \psi\right\rangle \\&=\left\langle\psi\left|i\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial k}, \frac{1}{k} \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}, \frac{1}{k \sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi}\right)\right| \psi\right\rangle \\&=\left(A_{k}, A_{\theta}, A_{\phi}\right) \\&=\left(0,0, \frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k}\right) \end{aligned} \]

5 贝里曲率

系统的贝里曲率为 \[ \begin{aligned} &F( k )=\nabla_{ k } \times A( k )\\ &=\frac{1}{k^{2} \sin \theta}\left(\begin{array}{ccc} e _{k} & q e _{\theta} & k \sin \theta e _{\phi} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial k} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} \\ A_{k} & k A_{\theta} & k \sin \theta A_{\phi} \end{array}\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{k \sin \theta}\left[\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(\sin \theta A_{\phi}\right)-\frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} A_{\theta}\right] e _{k}+\frac{1}{k}\left[\frac{1}{\sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} A_{k}-\frac{\partial}{\partial k}\left(k A_{\phi}\right)\right] e _{\theta}+\frac{1}{k}\left[\frac{\partial}{\partial k}\left(k A_{\theta}\right)-\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} A_{k}\right] e _{\phi}\\ &=\left(\frac{\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k}}{k} \cot \theta+\frac{1}{k} \frac{\partial\left(\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k}\right)}{\partial \theta}\right) e _{k}-\left(\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{\frac{2 k}{k}}+\frac{\partial\left(\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k}\right)}{\partial k}\right) e _{\theta} \\&=\left(\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k^{2}} \cot \theta+\frac{1}{4 k^{2}}\left(\sec ^{2} \frac{\theta}{2}\right)\right) e _{k}-\left(\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k^{2}}-\frac{\tan \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 k^{2}}\right) e _{\theta} \\&=\frac{1}{2 k^{2}} e _{k} \end{aligned} \] 小结 \[ \begin{aligned} F( k )&=\nabla_{ k } \times A( k ) \\&=\left(F_{k}, F_{\theta}, F_{\phi}\right)\\&=\left(\frac{1}{2 k^{2}}, 0,0\right) \end{aligned} \]

6 陈数

外尔点对应的陈数为 \[ \begin{aligned} C&=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{S} F d S \\&=\frac{1}{2 \pi} k^{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin \theta d \theta \int_{0}^{2 \pi} d \phi F_{k} \\&=\frac{1}{2 \pi} k^{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin \theta d \theta \int_{0}^{2 \pi} d \phi\left(\frac{1}{2 k^{2}}\right) \\&=1 \end{aligned} \] 同理，可以计算外尔点 \(H( k )=- k \cdot \sigma\) 对应的陈数为 \[ C=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{S} F d S=-1 \]

import numpy as np
from math import *
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt


def main():
    n =  0.5
    k1 = np.arange(-n*pi, n*pi, n/20)
    k2 = np.arange(-n*pi, n*pi, n/20)
    plot_bands_two_dimension(k1, k2, hamiltonian)


def hamiltonian(kx,kz,ky=0):  # Weyl semimetal
    A = 1
    M0 = 1
    M1 = 1
    H = A*(kx*sigma_x()+ky*sigma_y())+(M0-M1*(kx**2+ky**2+kz**2))*sigma_z()
    return H


def sigma_x():
    return np.array([[0, 1],[1, 0]])


def sigma_y():
    return np.array([[0, -1j],[1j, 0]])


def sigma_z():
    return np.array([[1, 0],[0, -1]])


def plot_bands_two_dimension(k1, k2, hamiltonian):
    from mpl_toolkits.mplot3d import Axes3D
    from matplotlib import cm
    from matplotlib.ticker import LinearLocator, FormatStrFormatter
    dim = hamiltonian(0, 0).shape[0]
    dim1 = k1.shape[0]
    dim2 = k2.shape[0]
    eigenvalue_k = np.zeros((dim2, dim1, dim))
    i0 = 0
    for k10 in k1:
        j0 = 0
        for k20 in k2:
            matrix0 = hamiltonian(k10, k20)
            eigenvalue, eigenvector = np.linalg.eig(matrix0)
            eigenvalue_k[j0, i0, :] = np.sort(np.real(eigenvalue[:]))
            j0 += 1
        i0 += 1
    fig = plt.figure()
    ax = fig.gca(projection='3d')
    k1, k2 = np.meshgrid(k1, k2)
    for dim0 in range(dim):
        ax.plot_surface(k1, k2, eigenvalue_k[:, :, dim0], cmap=cm.coolwarm, linewidth=0, antialiased=False)
    plt.xlabel('kx')
    plt.ylabel('kz') 
    ax.set_zlabel('E')  
    plt.show()


if __name__ == '__main__':
    main()

import numpy as np
from math import *
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt


def main():
    n =  0.5
    k1 = np.arange(-n*pi, n*pi, n/50)
    k2 = np.arange(-n*pi, n*pi, n/50)
    plot_bands_two_dimension_direct(k1, k2, hamiltonian)


def hamiltonian(kx,kz,ky=0):  # surface states of Weyl semimetal
    A = 1
    H = A*kx
    return H


def sigma_x():
    return np.array([[0, 1],[1, 0]])


def sigma_y():
    return np.array([[0, -1j],[1j, 0]])


def sigma_z():
    return np.array([[1, 0],[0, -1]])


def plot_bands_two_dimension_direct(k1, k2, hamiltonian):
    from mpl_toolkits.mplot3d import Axes3D
    from matplotlib import cm
    from matplotlib.ticker import LinearLocator, FormatStrFormatter
    fig = plt.figure()
    ax = fig.gca(projection='3d')
    dim1 = k1.shape[0]
    dim2 = k2.shape[0]
    eigenvalue_k = np.zeros((dim2, dim1))
    i0 = 0   
    for k10 in k1:
        j0 = 0
        for k20 in k2:
            if (k10**2+k20**2 <= 1):
                eigenvalue_k[j0, i0] = hamiltonian(k10, k20)
            else:
                eigenvalue_k[j0, i0] = 'nan'
            j0 += 1
        i0 += 1
    k1, k2 = np.meshgrid(k1, k2)
    ax.plot_surface(k1, k2, eigenvalue_k, cmap=cm.coolwarm, linewidth=0, antialiased=False) 
    plt.xlabel('kx')
    plt.ylabel('kz')
    ax.set_zlabel('E')  
    plt.show()


if __name__ == '__main__':
    main()
